Notable events
1 January - Chris uploads Light Powered Cwclight Reviews - Bumba-Lumba.
3 January - Reddit user JacksonStarship claims to have visited 14 Branchland Court and that Barb answered the door.[1]
4 January - Reddit user RandyMagnum68 photographs Chris and Flutter in Cary, North Carolina, picking up tickets for Animate! Raleigh.[2]
5 January - Chris and Flutter attend Animate! Raleigh.
6 January - Chris and Flutter get kicked out of Animate! Raleigh, due to weens reporting Chris.
7 January - Chris, coping with the ban, claims he had a nice weekend.[3]
11 January - CosmicKeyframe blocks Chris's Twitter account over his angry anti-My Little Pony G5 tweet.[4]
13 January - After being blocked by CosmicKeyframe, Chris writes a textwall about it.[5]
15 January
Focused, Divine Guidances - 01142024 is uploaded.
A user named "cwcvilleguardian" purchases a fanmade digital file of the Sonic Totem from website Cults3D.[6]
16 January - Chris records The Chris Chan Interview with transgender activist and content creator Keffals.[7]
17 January - OfficialCWCmart relists art commissions by Chris for sale.[8]
19 January
Chris responds to Twitter DMs by Ren Ryghts about the Dimensional Merge.[9]
OfficialCWCmart lists Books 0-3 of the Sonichu comic for sale.[10]
20 January
Chris livestreams Popular Request Game Stream.
OfficialCWCmart begins selling Sonic Totem figurines, allegedly using a digital file from Cults3D as the basis.[11]
21 January
Chris posts, ranting about My Little Pony G5[12] and gives advice on the idea of cosplaying as Barbara.[13]
Tinyhorse1942, a suspected sock account used by Chris on Twitter, begins posting.
22 January - Chris continues to lie about his Affair with Mother.[14]
23 January - Chris is briefly filmed at a Target in Lynchburg, VA.[15]
24 January - Chris uploads Esty Comment Review 2.
26 January - Chris uploads Past Ideas Reemerging - Part 3.
27 January - Chris chats in the Catboy Ranch Discord server.[16]
29 January - Chris announces a contest for fanfic writers and artists to send him their work.[17]
