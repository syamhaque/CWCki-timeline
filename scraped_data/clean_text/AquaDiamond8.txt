Before becoming a white-knight of Chris's, Aqua attempted to troll Chris by fabricating a story regarding his advances on a new gal-pal he had allegedly met named "Wendy" in March 2019, taking clear inspiration from Chris' interactions with Megan Schroeder. While posting as "Wendy", Aqua attempted to frame Chris for sexually harassing her, though Aqua also posted as a seperate person when posting about these interactions on Twitter.[5] Aqua posted the following:

Owls had also made a post on Kiwi Farms about people on Twitter telling Aqua to stop acussing Chris of these allegations.[7]

OCs
Like with her friend MKRNightVee, Aqua produced a number of OCs that would become prominent to Chris's lore. The characters that Aqua created originated from the Dimension ZX-49, which is dubbed as the "Macbook Pro Dimension".
CPU Aqua
AquaDiamond8 and Magi-Chan. Drawn by Aqua November 2019.

Aqua is the CPU of the Mac Book Pro computer. With her partner, Val Valentino, she does her best for everyone as she is able to. She has a bit of a temper and will rush into a situation if she feels her friends are in danger. Not sure if its a flaw, she's a little gullable [sic] from past mistakes, but she is learning from them.

In November 2019, during the Arbitarch Saga, in hopes to lure Chris away from Sockness, Aqua joined the Arbitarch CWC Alliance and embraced Chris's delusional beliefs, changing her Twitter handle to CPU Goddess Aqua of the Mac Book Pro. Later, Aqua began drawing fanart of her OC interacting with Magi-Chan.[11] She then posted a Pok√©mon drawing, claiming it to be an alt-dimension Magi-Chan:[12]
Chris had implemented the CPU Aqua character in a few of his Secret Shipfic cards.
Val Valentino
Aqua also created the OC Val Valentino as her interpretation of Magi-Chan, as well as CPU Aqua's companion. Aqua was the first person to play the character before passing the role off to the Watchmen who named themselves after them, Val.
On 12 December 2019, after ranting about ProjectSNT's redesign of Wild Sonichu, stating it looked like a Rokat, Aqua tried to discourage Chris from explaining the lore. Afterwards, Chris proceeded to roleplay as Val Valentino in an attempt to convince Aqua to draw fanart.[13]
Kylechu
Aqua also created Kylechu, the persona that would later be taken on by Watchman Kyle.
