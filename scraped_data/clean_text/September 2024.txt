September was the ninth month of 2024. Chris had a fairly active month, uploading new content to YouTube, launching the commerce/blogging website Cwclight, and taking another trip to Finland.

Notable events
1 September - Chris is spotted at a Walmart in Lynchburg and he takes photos with fans.[1]
4 September - Chris records himself reading customer reviews left on his Etsy store.[2]
6 September
The thirteenth commemoration of Bob's passing.
Chris uploads ETSY Reviews Sept 2024 to YouTube, which he recorded two days prior.
7 September - Chris posts a new page from Sonichu 0 HD.[3]
10 September
Chris posts photos of his art in support of Kamala Harris, which is displayed in the front yard of 14 Branchland Court.[4] Barb can be seen in the window above the dumpster in Chris's photo.
Chris does a livestream reacting to the presidential debate between Trump and Harris.[5]
12 September
Chris posts Welcome to Cwclight and Rules on Comments, his first two blogs on Cwclight.
Chris finishes drawing a commission.[6]
14 September - Chris uploads JCWCSP First Minecraft TRICKY TRIALS to YouTube, revealing three usernames of his new Minecraft friends.
16 September - Chris posts Sonichus, Rosechus, and more and Jesus Christ Chan Sonichu Prime to Cwclight.
18 September
Chris uploads MINECRAFT Cubepy Build Part 1 to YouTube.
The MINECRAFT Cubepy Build Part 2, Din Låt, MINECRAFT Cubepy Build Part 3, MINECRAFT Cubepy Build Part 4, and Minecraft Search for an Ender Portal videos are finished.
20 September - Chris uploads Introducing Cwclight - My new website to YouTube, announcing his brand new website Cwclight at officialsonichu.com.
24 September - Chris breaks his AI Chris Chan Vs Christ Chan hiatus by publishing his prerecorded take on Din Låt, a Swedish song.[7]
27 September
Chris is seen on security camera in Helsinki, Finland, identified by locals as a the store Normal at the Mall of Tripla.[8]
Chris publishes the second part to his Minecraft Cubepy build project.[9]
28 September - Chris and Flutter are spotted at the Mall of Tripla in Helsinki, Finland.[10]
