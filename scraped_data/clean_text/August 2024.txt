Notable events
1 August - The third anniversary of Chris's arrest.
6 August - Chris makes a tweet saying he does not approve of the fan game Christian Weston Chandler's Adult Chronicles.[1]
9 August - The first anniversary of Chris's case dismissal.
13 August - Chris and Flutter are spotted at River Ridge Mall.[2]
14 August - Chris's CwcvilleGuardian YouTube channel hits 100k subscribers[3] and he brags about it on Twitter.[4] Chris also says that YouTube owes him a YouTube Play Button.[5]
15 August - Chris makes tweets about reporting a G5 post because he doesn't like it.[6][7]
17 August
Chris starts up his public Tarot readings again.[8]
Bodycam footage of the 2021 incest arrest is released.[9]
19 August - Chris and Flutter are spotted at Valley View Mall. Chris was approached and a brief interaction was recorded. Later in the day, another photograph captures the first sight of Flutter's face since October 2023. [10]
22 August - Chris and Flutter are spotted at a Goodwill in Lynchburg.[11]
25 August
Chris is spotted at a Target in Lynchburg.[12]
Chris reveals that he plans to vote for Kamala Harris in the upcoming presidential election.[13]
27 August
Chris signs the Certificate of Authenticity for updated pictures for the Temple of Sonichu Sacred Rocks and Spiritual Talismans items.
Chris receives a new Tarot Deck which he waited months for.[14]
31 August - Chris uploads ITS DA BUTTON, a video celebrating receiving his Silver Play Button from YouTube.
