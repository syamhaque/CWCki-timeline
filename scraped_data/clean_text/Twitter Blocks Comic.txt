Title: Twitter Blocks Comic
URL: https://sonichu.com/cwcki/Twitter_Blocks_Comic
Categories: TF2 Analysis Saga, My Little Pony, Sonichu comic book issues
================================================================================

During January 2019, a perfect storm of a campaign by trolls and Chris's own reputation resulted in Chris being blocked by several high-profile creators from the MLP brony fandom, in particular members of the TF2 Analysis show.
Chris produced this comic as his reaction to the rejection and published it on 29 January.[1] In the pages, Magi-Chan talks to Dr. Wolf, a creator of the TF2 Analysis show, about Chris's feelings on the matter. Magi-chan and Dr. Wolf have a long text-wall conversation about how getting blocked on Twitter is hurting Chris, that he is getting harassed by being blocked, that people shouldn't ignore him, and that creators need to reconnect with their OCs.
