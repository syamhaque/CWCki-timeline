NOTE: Chris was not directly involved in this conversation.
The Watchmen occasionally discussed the Youtuber Alexander Gordon Jahans in the second layer of their Discord server The Place, leading up to scheming to have Chris and Jahans interact.
The Watchman Naught has an interest in Jahans, also known as Farshnuke, posting threads on him on both the Kiwi Farms[1] and Onion Farms[2] forums. MKR also has an interest, posting about him in The Place, and in March 2021, tried to convince Chris that Jahans could help him in Chris's quest of role-playing as a psychic detective.[3] However, no sign of Jahans agreeing to talk to Chris has emerged.
The Watchman Kyle repeatedly expressed disapproval towards the attempt to have Chris and Jahans interact.
