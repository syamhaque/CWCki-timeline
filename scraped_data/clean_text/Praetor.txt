Praetor LLC is a group of carpetbaggers and enablers. They first came into contact with Chris in 2020 with the aim of producing and selling Sonichu and Chris-themed products on their Etsy store OfficialCWCmart with Chris's permission, giving him a cut of the profits. In December 2020, Praetor began offering Sonichu medallions for sale, which were produced by the group and approved of by Chris. Praetor has since expanded to other, more bizarre, products, some produced by the group, and others by Chris. Chris's income split for products made by him is unknown. As of 2023, OfficialCWCmart has generated more than 3000 sales, giving them a minimum of between $49,905 and $116,000 in revenue, although their actual revenue is unknown but potentially higher.[2] While the sales have slowed down considerably, by March 2024, the number has increased to over 4200.
During the Jail Saga, Chris continued communicating with them, mailing them art from jail, which they added to OfficialCWCmart.
Upon Chris's being released from jail, they have continued their involvement. In June 2023, Praetor member Caden Peck was involved with financing a Big Island, VA house, which Chris moved into later that year. During The Chris Chan Interview, Chris mentioned paying rent. Chris has uploaded or live-streamed several highly edited videos which all promote OfficialCWCmart.

Before Chris
Caden, Owen, Annie, and Cabell were all students at Goochland High School in Goochland, Virginia in the late 2010s.
Caden had claimed that Praetor has been around since 2017, working with "creative types."[8]. If this is true, Caden would have been 14 or 15 years old at the time of Praetor's creation. Caden also claimed that they worked with Kevin Baugh, founder of the Republic of Molossia (no relation), and wrote a dissertation for him in order to officiate Molossia as a sovereign nation.[8]
Praetor used to have a dedicated website, but was taken down by Caden due to the domain costs. He decided that instead of waiting to have "eccentric people" find him, he would personally reach out to them himself.[8] Naught once claimed that Praetor went after other lolcows before Chris, including Tommy Tooter, whom they tried to make products for.[9]

Royalty payment
On 7 December 2020, Praetor began offering Sonichu medallions for sale on OfficialCWCmart; the medallions were produced by the group and approved of by Chris. In exchange, Chris was paid a royalty for the use of his Sonichu IP.
A month later, the Watchmen noticed that OfficialCWCmart had made $5,900 worth of sales revenue on their medallion product, also the store's only product at the time.[14] In mid-January 2021, Chris told the Watchmen he had received nearly $400 in small cash amounts for his share.[13] This equates to at least a 6.7% royalty (possibly more as date of Chris's payment is unknown). While royalty rates can vary, Chris's rate falls within a typical range.[15]
Other information is unknown, such as Praetor's operating costs for the medallions and how much profit the group members had after expenses.
The Watchmen - among them MKRNightVee and Naught - erroneously believed that Chris was entitled to "almost all" of Praetor's revenue for the medallions.[14] In the Taser Discussion call from February 2021, MKR and Naught complained to Praetor's Caden. Chris himself did not mind, again pointing out he had been paid $400.

Taser Controversy
See also: TaserÂ and Taser Discussion
On 15 November 2020, Praetor posted a screenshot of an online shopping service's shopping cart - in the cart was a Pikachu mask and a VIPERTEK VTS-989 560 BV stun gun (they later claimed the image was for promotion of sales of autographed "tasers".)
In February 2021, Chris stated that he wanted to borrow one of Praetor's tasers to stun his Gate Crystal and Celestial Key quartz crystals in an attempt to activate the Dimensional Merge.[17] The Watchmen confronted Caden about this, saying that it was dangerous for the organization to allow Chris to use a taser, though Caden insisted that Chris would be safe by using a less powerful version of the taser. Talks broke down when Watchmen members began insulting Caden on Discord, with him returning the favor; all parties including Chris agreed to have a call on the issue. During the call, one of the Watchmen - Val - sarcastically asked about Chris tazing himself. Chris half-jokingly replied that he would. Caden objected and reiterated it would be a bad idea.
The Watchmen members MKRNightVee and Naught then chose to spread lies about the potential taser use. In particular, MKRNightVee falsely claimed that the taser was meant to be used on Chris.
Sales
A message left for a customer who bought a taser
In 2022, Praetor began selling tasers previously autographed by Chris on OfficialCWCmart, supposedly for the purpose of simulating "zapping to the extreme" for buyers.
The tasers had originally been meant for sale, but were temporarily shelved due to some members of the Watchmen instigating drama and spreading lies over the taser.[18]
However, Etsy quickly removed the listing. Eventually, Praetor created a listing titled 'The Secret Listing', a listing that had no information on what it was.[19] It was later revealed that The Secret Listing was a taser, with the fact hidden in order to bypass Etsy guidelines.[20]
One of the people who bought the taser would mail it to the channel SuperMega, where they would unbox it on video.[21]

Post-jail involvement
See also: OfficialCWCmart#Post-Jail Saga Items
After plenty of sightings online and offline, Chris finally made his grand return in October 2023. Along with Chris's return, OfficialCWCmart returned as well, stocked with a plethora of new items. This included new Sonichu medallions, with brand new pictures of Chris in front of a green screen, new promotions of Neo Spiritual Christianity, and dozens upon dozens of lost commissions that Chris drew for the Everfree Northwest fundraiser in 2021
Chris's new videos from this point forward were incredibly high quality (for Chris's standards), making it very likely that Praetor was behind these videos, as they have had previous experience with high-quality editing and Chris uses the same green screen which is used in the Etsy store. Chris also consistently promoted OfficialCWCmart in the description of these videos. The list of products has later further increased in January 2024, though they appear to be the same overpriced low quality stuff as before. This has also expanded to art commissions for a totally reasonable sum of $225-300.
In June 2023, Caden Peck and Essential Estate LLC bought a property in Big Island, VA where Chris currently lives and pays rent.
