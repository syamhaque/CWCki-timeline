Ongoing
The things described in this article are still happening, or are still being looked into. As a result, it may be subject to frequent change, and the information provided may not be entirely reliable.
Light Powered Cwclight Reviews are a series of videos that began on 1 January 2024.
Here Chris tries to review the toys used to create his custom-built Sonichu Transformers as well as promoting his build.
Chris tries to copy emgo316, ripping off his intro style of using a toy representation to give a blurb of the toy before smash cutting to the video title with a theme song.
He also promotes BigBadToyStore without permission.
