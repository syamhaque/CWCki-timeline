Highlights
5 December - Chris uploads three videos, one criticising his mother and two reaching out to FunkO's cereal.
6 December - Trolls order items off Chris's Amazon wishlist and cancel their order, making them disappear off of the list and leading Chris to believe he's getting lots of toys for Christmas. Chris throws a fit at Amazon on Twitter after discovering this.
11 December - CWCSonichu leaks another Idea Guy video: the Tour of Chris's House III. Chris has another Therapeutic Docket appointment.
13 December - Chris buys a Treadmill from Goodwill.
17 December - CWCSonichu rants on Twitter about the upcoming Sonic the Hedgehog movie, one of the reasons being the blue arms, and says that he (and the "real" Sonic the Hedgehog) are not amused.
18 December - Chris tells the Teen Troon Squad about how he had damaged the Amnyfest Ring.[1]
19 December -
Chris is ordered to pay $2,777,90 to Midland Funding for unpaid debts plus $55 in court costs, making this Chris's only court hearing this year that didn't end in a continuance.
22 December - In a long rant on Twitter, CWCSonichu tries to justify why the Merge hasn't occurred yet. Notably, he encourages his audience to pray to him.
25 December
C-Mas.
Marvin[2] poses as Magi-Chan on Chris's Twitter and doxes Lukas.[3]
26 December - Chris describes a meditation session to the Teen Troon Squad.[4]
27 December - MKRNightVee begins tweeting. She claims that the Dimensional Merge is coming, promotes her NightVee comic,[5] and claims to have powers in a Twitter reply to Chris.[6]
31 December
For the year 2018, Chris makes $10,745.93 in sales revenue from eBay.[7]
