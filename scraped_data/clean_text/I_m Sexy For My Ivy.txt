"I'm Sexy For My Ivy" is Christian and the Hedgehog Boys's version of Justin Timberlake's "SexyBack", and is (thankfully) the last track from Christian and the Hedgehog Boys COMEBACK!.
This is standard fare from Chris, the new lyrics frequently break the rhyming scheme of the original and are sung out of time. He also inconsistently uses some of the original lines, causing much of the song to make no sense.
