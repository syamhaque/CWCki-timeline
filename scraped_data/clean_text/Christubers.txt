A Christuber is someone on YouTube who is known for creating content that is centered around Christian Weston Chandler.
Some Christubers also tend to be carpetbaggers, taking advantage of Chris's online notoriety in order to grow their channels through dishonest means.

Ben Saint
Main article: Ben Saint
Ben Saint is a webcomic artist and self-proclaimed "Dimensional Prophet," who exploited Chris's internet infamy to boost his own work. Despite initial conflicts and accusations of fictional genocide, their interactions became friendlier by late 2019, with Chris even appearing in a livestream and supporting Saint on Patreon before his arrest in 2021.
Beneath the Bridge
Main article: Beneath the Bridge
Beneath the Bridge was a YouTube series by Smoky Chris and ThatSperg, posted on SomeChannellllllll, focused on examining the trolls who influenced Chris’s life. The series ran from February to December 2019 before being abandoned, covering various figures from Sarah Hammer to Jimmy Hill, and Chris later shared his emotional reactions to watching the episodes on Megan Schroeder.
Copitz
Main article: Copitz
Copitz is a YouTuber and speedrunner, who started visiting Chris Chan in 2018, filming videos of their interactions and local outings. He also helped organize Chris's interview for The Chris Chan Conspiracy docuseries and later released additional footage on his YouTube channel.
Dillin Thomas
Main article: Dillin Thomas
Dillin Thomas is a YouTuber known for creating content around the Sonic the Hedgehog series and for reading and commenting on Chris-related material from the CWCki. He exploited Chris's imagery by selling a "Sonichu and Rosechu Valentine's Day" shirt, later leaking a direct message conversation with Chris about the legitimacy of the Incest Call, which led to Chris calling him a "minor traitor" after his arrest.
Ethan Ralph
Main article: Ethan Ralph
Ethan Ralph is a Podcaster and Live streamer who exploited the fallout from the Chris Chan incest revelations by attempting to interview Chris and livestreaming his arrest. He also tried to profit by selling t-shirts with Chris's likeness without permission or compensation.
Geno Samuel
Main article: Geno Samuel
Geno Samuel is a YouTuber and musician, who gained attention for his Chris Chan: A Comprehensive History series and for selling Chris-themed merchandise without permission. Despite Chris's objections to some of his products, such as the "WANT WOMAN!" shirt, Geno continued to profit from these sales.
GiBi
Main article: GiBi
GiBi is a YouTuber who makes content focusing on Chris Chan, who gained popularity with his weekly updates. His channel, originally centered on Star Wars and gaming, shifted toward Chris-related videos, and he was involved in promoting a petition to get Chris on Dr. Phil. Despite rebranding his channel multiple times, GiBi continues to document Chris's activities, viewing his life as "real life fiction" and a cautionary tale.
PewDiePie
Main article: PewDiePie
PewDiePie is a popular Swedish YouTuber, who has over 100 million subscribers. In 2017, PewDiePie featured Chris in two videos, discussing the Sonic fandom and briefly covering Chris's life.
The WCT
Main article: The WCT
The WCT is a former member of the Watchmen group and amateur documentarian who created works on dark topics and claimed significant influence in the CWC sphere. His actions, including doxing conflicts and enabling Chris's delusions, led to controversy and criticism, ultimately forcing him to retreat from the internet.
TheGamerFromMars
Main article: The Chris Chan Conspiracy
TheGamerFromMars is a YouTuber who created The Chris Chan Conspiracy docuseries, covering the life of Chris, including previously unseen footage from 2019 that offers insights into his daily life and delusions. In 2024, TheGamerFromMars met with Chris again to work on a fifth episode, noting Chris appeared more mentally stable, and confirmed that an update is in progress.
Xleepy
Main article: Xleepy
Xleepy is a MilSim YouTuber, who collaborated with Chris, likely due to his friendship with fellow YouTuber Copitz. After featuring Chris demonstrating his supposed psychic powers in a video, Xleepy quickly removed the footage and re-uploaded a version with Chris's scene edited out without explanation.
