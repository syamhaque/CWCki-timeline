Delusion disclosure: This article or section takes place during Chris's phase of role-playing as Sonichu. He may refer to his Christine Chandler personality and physical body in the third person by using terms such as "mama".

August was the eighth month of 2020. The month was most notable for having a number of leaks provided by the Watchmen while Chris himself answered some questions through Discord. The COVID-19 pandemic also continued this month.
Chris at a D&D meeting online, 1 August 2020

Highlights
2 August - A scrapped interview from September 2017, between Chris and the Internet Dream Lounge, is leaked.[1]
6 August - Chris's Last Will and Testament is recorded; he announces that, upon leaving for dimension C-197, his possessions are to be left to Meghan Kathleen Ringo and Kevin Fairchild, and that Barb is to allow Kevin to move into the house.[2]
10 August
The Watchman Naught joins the Official CWCki Server and leaks a large number of images, ranging from pictures of conversations between Chris and various fans to new pictures of Chris's room.[3] Chris also joins the Discord at Naught's request[4] and posts in it.[5]
In The Place Discord server, Chris asks Val and Naught for input on what to say to CWCki Server members. While browsing the CWCki Server, Chris finds a leaked letter he had sent to Kai earlier in the year and is annoyed at the leak.[6]
11 August - The full version of Tour of Chris's House III is leaked.
14 August - Chris is banned from the Official CWCki Server.[7]
16 August - Chris posts on Twitter about replacing an air conditioner and wearing a du-rag [8]
22 August
Chris answers a Q&A over Discord.[9]
Reddit user EdBoySenpai takes a photo with Chris at Sheetz.[10]
26 August
Chris's application for his temple to be considered a Pokéstop in Pokémon GO is rejected.[citation needed]
Chris responds to a provoking DM by Ben Saint, using role-playing to deny Ben's claim that CWCville is being attacked.[11]
27 August - Chris takes a break from being possessed by Sonichu, though roleplays instead that he is Chris of an alt-dimension 1214, and he answered a few more questions for the ongoing Q&A.[12]
28 August
Chris (of alt-dimension 1214) complains about a Dillin Thomas video and tries to encourage Kyle to link with alt-dimension Kyle so he might build a dimensional portal device. Chris then switches back to role-playing as Sonichu.[13]
More of the ongoing Q&A is answered in a call.[14]
30 August - Fans discover that Chris's CWCville Shopping website is shut down. No announcement is made by him.
31 August - The court file on Chris's trespass after being forbidden charge from 2018, in which he repeatedly signs his name as "Chris Chan Sonichu", is posted on Kiwi Farms.[15]
