Chris in May.
May is the fifth month of 2024. Chris apparently spent most of the month in Finland, flying home on the 24th with a layover at Dallas Fort Worth International Airport, where he was caught on camera by an observer. Chris also revealed that he has security cameras installed at his house in Big Island which captured photos of a car that had followed him and showed up to his house.
