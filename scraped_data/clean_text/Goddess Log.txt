================================================================================

The Goddess Log is a journal Chris has kept in Central Virginia Regional Jail while held on his charge of incest. It was mentioned in several of Chris's letters from jail. Chris's 27 November 2021 letter to Kenneth Engelhardt mentions that he began keeping the journal on or before 7 August 2021, although it is not known when he began calling it the "Goddess Log".[1] The logs are separated into eight known volumes, and each consist of roughly 100 pages. Whenever Chris wrote enough, he would send the pages off to 14 Branchland Court for Barbara to look after. This resulted in the fifth volume being lost due to irrevocable water damages that occurred under her care.
Chris mentions several eccentric/insane inmates that are also in the protective custody unit, like a woman who screamed and smeared her cell with feces, or a guy who bangs on his door and makes rooster calls.
Chris also makes mention in a November 2021 letter of the Akashic records, a compendium of all universal thoughts, events, words, and emotion recorded for all time in the religion of Theosophy. Chris claims to have gotten several of the things he has recorded in the logs from the Akashic Guardian, who is the scribe of these records.
Chris was released from jail on 27 March 2023. In an October 2023 livestream, he mentioned that his crew was working on transcribing the Goddess Log's pages for publication.[2] According to Praetor the Goddess Log is hundreds of pages long and must be censored to remove names and addresses within it.[3]
On 9 February 2024, Chris read nine days worth of passages from the Goddess Log starting from the date 5 February 2022.[4]
On 20 September 2024, the newly released Cwclight website revealed excerpts from the Goddess Log dated 23 November 2022.[5]
On 11 April 2025, TheGamerFromMars revealed that he has full access to the Goddess Logs, and showed excerpts in part 5 of The Chris Chan Conspiracy.
