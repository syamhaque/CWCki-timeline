He also bows like a woman, mispronounces Miyamoto's name, and redundantly addresses him as Mr. Miyamoto-san, which is essentially "Mr. Mr. Miyamoto."
Chris also verifies his identity with several unimpeachable methods:
He says he is the real Christian Weston Chandler and asks that the viewer believe him.
He holds up his driver's license, proving that he is someone who might be able to obtain a fake ID.
He uses his fingers to obscure his license number, but also manages to cover his name, address, and other information.
He shows off his custom-painted Wii controllers, which everyone knows only the real CWC could possess.
