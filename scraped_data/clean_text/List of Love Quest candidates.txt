The Women (and Men) in Chris's Life
Sarah Hammer
Sarah was the first person that Chris tried to bond with, as well as Chris' first manipulator, even before the Internet. Once a gal-pal of his, Sarah Hammer was a childhood "friend" of Chris who tricked him into crawling into a tight, dark, cobweb-infested space, and tried to convince him to eat a honeysuckle. Her relationship with another lover was also part of the reason as to why Chris began his Love Quest. When Wes Iseli started dating her, Chris felt there was a romantic rivalry between the two of them, even though Sarah had little concern about Chris at the time. Wes, enviously, barely even knew Chris. His pursuit of Sarah came to an end when she got married (Chris was not invited to the wedding, but attended "in spirit") and he found another girl to crush over.
Tiffany Robinson
Tiffany is a former gal pal of Chris's from the days he lived in Richmond, and was Chris's first crush. In Chris's words, "we would hang out now and then during school hours and play. Then in the 4th Grade, she dumped me for some other boy. I do not know much of what she has been up to these days".[1] In 2021, Chris said that she lived "somewhere beyond Standardsville" [sic] and that he had a crush on her.[2] Her current whereabouts, or if she is aware of Chris anymore, are unknown.
Natasha Turner
Natasha is a former gal pal Chris met at Providence Middle School. They were next-door neighbors for a while, often hanging out at the bus stop near their homes. She was slightly older than him and a "good girl". For a while he had a crush on her until he saw her smoking: "That was It for me right there".[3][4]
Laura
During his sophomore year, Chris began pursuing Laura Dorazio, a cheerleader. Laura was Chris's first major high school crush. Despite being told that she merely saw him as a "friend", Chris's infatuation would continue even after graduating high school; he habitually pored over old yearbooks with pictures of her. In several ways, his feelings towards her foreshadowed behaviors he'd show with many of the candidates that appear later on this list. Chris appeared to have taken no for an answer, but still obsessed over her, mentioning her in several future videos and comics.
Kellie
Kellie Andes was another high school crush of Chris's starting in his sophomore year, being Chris's lab partner in his chemistry class. Chris considers her to be his "High School Sweetheart", and believed that he had a shot with her, but blew it as a result of na√Øvet√© or general inattentiveness with proposing to her. Unlike most other high school gal-pals, Chris was able to continue contact with Kellie for an entire year after graduating. The stories as to what came of this are highly conflicting, but Chris would attempt to call her weekly in vain. Despite Chris's belief that he had a "shot" with her, he stood no chance whatsoever. Kellie had already been happily married to a firefighter with two children. Kellie cut off all ties from Chris as soon as she discovered the video where Chris simulates licking Jackie's vagina.
Tiffany
Tiffany Gowen was yet another High School gal-pal that Chris developed feelings for. Compared to other high-school gal pals, Tiffany has shown the most compassion towards Chris. Chris sat at her lunch table everyday and she attended his 18th birthday party. After learning that Chris brought his mom to prom as his date, Tiffany asked Chris to dance out of pity, as well as being the only student to console him during his graduation. All of this kindness directed towards him made Chris consider Tiffany to be a potential sweetheart. Like most of his other gal pals at the time, communication between the two ended after high school. Despite this, Chris was still convinced that were they to meet again he would have a shot. Chris expands on these fantasies in "The High School Story" about the prom dance with genderswapped counterparts of Tiffany and himself, which is followed by the cross gendered Tiffany proposing marriage. Efforts by Chris to track down Tiffany had intensified by 2013, up until the troll Thetan had impersonated several high-school gal-pals and convinced him that there were no mutual feelings between the two of them.
Sarah Anne Bevel
Sarah Anne Bevel is a former gal-pal who was classmates with Chris at high school. She and Kellie were Chris's lab partners in chemistry class, sat with him at lunch, and attended his 18th birthday party. Sarah and her boyfriend were the only couple Chris was aware of during high school, who stated that "It was fun to just to watch them flirt with each other; I've could've learned from that, but my Autism and normal social phobias held me back then."[5] Like his other school gal-pals, he lost contact with Sarah after graduating, with her even stating she no longer knew Chris when he tried to recontact her years later.[6] Eventually, Sarah (really Thetan in disguise) informed Chris that his gal-pals had only acted like Chris's friends because "we felt sorry for him", in an attempt to try and get Chris to leave her alone.

Molly is a former high school classmate of Chris. She was a cheerleader during Chris's Freshman Year. She shared a few classes with Chris. According to Chris, it was "the ONLY match-up I felt was a good match-up at all back then".[7]
