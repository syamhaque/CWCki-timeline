MINECRAFT Cubepy Build is a series of videos uploaded to Chris's YouTube channel that were originally recorded some time in August 2024 and edited and first published in 18 September 2024 with later parts published in the subsequent three weeks.
In the videos, Chris works on a project that entails building a giant Cubepy within Minecraft.
All of the video have the following description:
I went into my Minecraft, and I started a fun build project that required clays and lots of copper to make.
A Cubepy (aka Green Cube Peepy).
This project and video was not endorsed by ItemLabel.
