"All I Want For Christmas Is a Pretty Girlfriend" is a music video filmed by Chris on 20 December 2004 to promote his Love Quest. It is the earliest known music video that Chris has made.
The rare B-side follows his performance of "So Need a Cute Girl", which he begins singing shortly before the five-minute mark. It is noteworthy for being the earliest known footage of Chris wearing both his clown shirt and one of his medallions.
