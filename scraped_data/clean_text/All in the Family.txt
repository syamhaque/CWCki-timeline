All in the Family is an American sitcom involving the hijinks of a prejudiced man named Archie Bunker and his family, airing on CBS from 1971 to 1979. It is one of the many ancient sitcoms Chris (and likely his father) enjoyed.
Mentions by Chris
Chris listed the show, along side his other interests, on his MySpace page.
While doing a Jamaican impression to Clyde Cash, Chris blurted out, "if it fits Archie Bunker's shoes, then let it be!"
Chris compared Gregg Mays and Clyde Cash to be "about as fake and fictional" as actors merely playing characters, bringing up Archie Bunker and George Jefferson as examples (also acknowledging the spinoff show The Jeffersons).
Lars compared Sonichu to Archie Comics, then randomly brought up Archie Bunker, but Chris didn't seem to notice or care.
Chris mentioned being a fan of the actor who played George Jefferson, proving that he isn't racist.
Parallels to Chris
The plot of a season 7 episode involves an electrical fire breaking out in the bathroom during the middle of the night. It's unknown whether or not Chris has seen this particular episode.
