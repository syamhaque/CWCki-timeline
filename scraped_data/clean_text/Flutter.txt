Flutter (born 1992 or 1993) is the nickname Chris gave to a female friend of his. Flutter reached out to Chris at some point during his time in Central Virginia Regional Jail. Sometime after Chris was released in March 2023, he met up with her in the city of Lynchburg, Virginia, and they began hanging out together.[1] Chris has referred to her simply as a friend, suggesting that their relationship is not romantic, although he does not explicitly deny having romantic feelings for her either. Chris also claims that she is thirty years old (as of 17 October 2023), despite her childlike appearance. The nickname "Flutter" comes from her fondness for the My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic character Fluttershy.
After Chris's release from jail, he was frequently photographed by people who managed to spot him in public. In September 2023, he was spotted with Flutter for the first time. On the 17th, she was spotted allegedly making out with Chris at a CVS, causing many people to believe she was his girlfriend. Otaku King also claimed that the two of them were a couple. In Setting the Record straight - there never was any sex involving me in 2021, however, Chris simply refers to her as a friend. Some observers have speculated that Flutter may be Finnish or of Finnish descent, noting that Chris began studying Finnish intensively on Duolingo since October 2023 for no apparent reason.[2] This theory was bolstered by sightings of Chris and Flutter in Finland. A Reddit user who saw Flutter claims that she did not have an accent.[3] Another Redditor claims that she spoke in a fake anime girl voice.[4]
Flutter's identity and motives remain one of the more mysterious aspects of modern Christory. With both Chris and Flutter herself seemingly unwilling to divulge any information about her, as well as a lack of any known social media presence, most discussion regarding her has been purely speculative. This has allowed for the proliferation of numerous theories and rumors. Theories ranged from things such as Flutter being underage due to her youthful and diminutive appearance, being a recruit from Praetor meant to keep Chris in check, being an obscure Finnish lolcow mostly unknown to Chris's primarily North American follower base, to even the possibility of being impregnated by Chris. Time will tell as to whether or not any of these questions end up being answered, but for now, Chris seems content maintaining his silence on the matter.

When he first spoke about her shortly after returning to the Internet, Chris only shared her nickname and the fact that she had reached out to him while he was incarcerated, and that they had met up in Lynchburg following his release.
In a May 2025 call with a ween, Chris was asked directly about Flutter and proceeded to lie about everything possibly related to her, including denying that he ever went to Finland with her or was learning Finnish on Duolingo.
Instead he claimed Flutter lives in Philadelphia and is going to college there, but was both unwilling and unable to provide any details when pressed further.
He refused to give up her real name or even the first letter, even after being offered bribes of $50, $150 and purchases from his Etsy store, stating simply, "Her name shall not be revealed."[33]
