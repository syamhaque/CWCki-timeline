"Punchy and Layla's Dance In The Dark" is the penultimate song on Christian and the Hedgehog Boys COMEBACK!. It is Chris's tribute for Punchy Sonichu and Layla Flaaffy, to the tune of Bruce Springsteen's "Dancing in the Dark". Towards the end, Chris reveals that Punchy and Layla apparently like either S&M, the donkey punch, or fisting. It's hard to tell, really.
