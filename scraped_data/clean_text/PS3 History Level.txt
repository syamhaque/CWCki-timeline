PS3 History Level is a LittleBigPlanet level Chris made during his period of solitary confinement in November 2008. On 25 November 2008, Chris uploaded "LBC1-CWC's Playstation 3 History", showcasing his level. The level was created in response to a contest Media Molecule was holding called the "LittleBigChallenge", the goal of which was to create a level celebrating the second anniversary of the PS3. Never one to turn down a contest, Chris made a level that sucked up to the system, and it was promptly featured on the LBP site.[1] The level did not win the contest, but did earn an honorable mention, which granted Chris the Metal Gear Solid level pack for free.
Chris came back into the limelight of the community when his nudes were leaked on the LBP forum.
Note that Chris mistakenly claims he bought the PS3 in March 2006, when he actually purchased it in 2007.
