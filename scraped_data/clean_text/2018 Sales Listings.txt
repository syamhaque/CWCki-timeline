This page lists the stuff Chris offered for sale in 2018. In addition, he also sold Sonichu comics printed through Lulu via Patreon.
Chris hit his highest amount of sales revenue during this year, exceeding over $10,000 in sales. The sales were heavily influenced by the Idea Guys saga, as they were alleged to have extorted Chris out of $6,000 by threatening to destroy his imaginary city CWCville. To help raise money to pay them off, Chris sold many items, including several of his Transformers toys, his Amnyfest Ring and ShecameforCWC.JPG, as well as random objects from the Chandler home including Cole Smithey's old homework assignments.
Sales continued after April, when the Idea Guys were removed from power, as the Chandlers were still having financial issues. Chris sold more items during the summer such as Sonichu- and Chris Chan-themed autographed photos, cards, and Fanta cans.
Sales from this year would also start the Guard Dogs and Teen Troon Squad sagas, as Guard Dog The Captain began contact with Chris via purchasing his Amnyfest Ring, and Teen Troon Squad members began contact by purchasing shecameforCWC.JPG.
