================================================================================

Weston is a surname (or middle name) that has belonged to the following individuals:
Daniel Weston, Barbara's supposed ancestor.
Francis Weston, one of Barbara's actual ancestors
Barbara Chandler, born as Barbara Ann Weston.
Our protagonist Christian Weston Chandler, who has retained "Weston" as his middle name throughout his life.
