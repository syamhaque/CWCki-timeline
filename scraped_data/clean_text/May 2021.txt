Chris and Barb in May 2021
Chris showing off his COVID-19 vaccination card in May 2021
May was the fifth month of 2021. Chris uploaded a video of himself covering the song "I'll Be Your Mirror" by The Velvet Underground this month, and uploaded a bizarre audio clip to Twitter in which he made odd vocalizations. Aside from this, Barb's debt hearing was continued to July 2021.
Highlights
4 May - Chris releases a seductive image of Chris-chan Sonichu on social media.[1]
9 May - Chris celebrates Mother's Day.[2]
11 May - Chris resumes messaging The WCT on Discord.[3]
17 May - Chris gets his second dose of the Pfizer COVID-19 vaccination.
19 May - Barb's debt hearing is continued to 14 July 2021.[citation needed]
19 May - Chris announces on social media that he and Barb have been given COVID-19 vaccinations.[4]
20 May - Chris releases a new photo of a Sonichu medallion on Instagram, stating that he had just applied "a fresh bit of paint and epoxy covering."[5]
20 May - Chris releases a bizarre audio clip of him vocalizing.
26 May - Chris shows his copy of Warhol / Chris Chan.[6]
26 May - Chris posts a bizzare series of tweets ranting about his failed relationships and trolls.[7]
27 May - Chris uploads a video of himself covering the song "I'll Be Your Mirror" by The Velvet Underground.
29 May - Chris announces that the prices of the meditation crystals and enchanted rocks on OfficialCWCmart are lowered from $45 to $20.[8]
31 May - Don Lashomb, author of Warhol / Chris Chan, is interviewed over Reddit by a CWCki editor.[9]
