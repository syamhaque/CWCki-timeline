Chris on 25 February.
February was the second month of 2016. Chris spent most of the month e-begging for a PS4. He also made a video apologizing to those with Aspergers about his previous offensive remarks towards them, was interviewed by CatKnight for the third time, and made a bizarre video showing off a collection of his own semen. Chris also turned 34 this month. Meanwhile, Barbara was sued by Discover Bank due to failing to pay her debts.

Events of February 2016
1 February
Chris announces on Facebook that he has ended his embargo of GameStop, Best Buy, and Simon Malls, after realizing his actions haven't had any actual social or economic impact.
The infamous Yep, I'm on TV :) disc is listed on Etsy, and remained there unsold for the entire month.
Chris makes a Facebook post announcing special Valentine's Day deals for his Etsy products. He offers to trade merchandise for a new PS4.
2 February - The Kiwi Farms member CatKnight releases a third interview with Chris.
3 February - Chris provides links to the released CatKnight interviews on Facebook.
9 February - Chris makes a post on Facebook stating that he feels dizzy.
11 February - Chris uses Facebook to once again beg his fans to gift him a PS4.
12 February
Chris makes a Facebook post defending his previous PS4 e-begging.
Chris posts an apology video on YouTube, asking forgiveness for past offenses against males and individuals with Asperger's. He yet again demands a PS4 for his birthday in the video.
14 February - Valentine's Day. Nothing happens.
15 February
Chris announces on Facebook that Sonichu Book 4 is now available for sale.
Chris makes excuses on Facebook for not delivering on his promise to write Sonichu pages after having received $1,000 for that very purpose.
16 February - Chris records and uploads a Youtube video congratulating a fan for coming out out of the closet.
17 February - Chris releases an update video in which he once again asks for a PS4, shows off his Amiibos, and praises Hillary Clinton.
19 February - Discover Bank files a lawsuit against Barb for failing to pay her debts.
20 February - Chris complains on Facebook that a focus on online media is causing people to lose social skills.
22 February - Chris states on Facebook that he enjoyed the new PSP advertisements.
23 February - Chris complains on Facebook about his health and "life problems".
24 February - Chris turns 34. He celebrates by watching an Aqua Teen Hunger Force video and wishing happy birthday to himself on Facebook in Spanish.
25 February - Chris uploads a video whining about how nobody bought him a PS4 for his birthday.
26 February
Chris posts a new, even more confusing offer on Facebook in order to persuade someone to send him a free PS4.
Chris records a bizarre video in which he shows off multiple samples of his own semen that he stores in his refrigerator freezer.
27 February - The video from the previous day is leaked on to the Kiwi Farms by Marvin.
28 February
Chris provides a link on Facebook to his video from 25 February.
Chris once again complains about Jeff/Francine in a video, calling his unauthorized merchandise "low-brow" and "offensive."
Chris records and uploads a video "celebrating" the 20th anniversary of the Pokemon franchise. Chris only actually mentions Pokemon a few times before ranting about things related to Sonichu and then quickly mentions South Park before the video ends.
29 February - Chris makes a Facebook post railing against those who complained about his earlier efforts to get a PS4.
