Etymology
On 9 March 2021, Chris sent Anaxis a handful of messages he requested him to chronicle, viewing Anaxis as the "one true channeler".[2] Chris regarded the messages as "guidance" he received from a "very insightful medium", who Anaxis would later learn was Molly the Medium. On the 13th of that month, Chris, assuming that Anaxis was still friendly with the other Watchmen and that they knew he was still talking with him, made the announcement about Anaxis' new chronicling, declaring how they're aware of coming onto it from an "Anaxis bird".[3]
What Chris had likely meant when he referred to the "Anaxis bird" was, under the assumption that Anaxis told the other Watchmen about the upcoming Chronicling, that Anaxis was meant to record the information that Chris requested that he put in A Medium's Guidance, one of the Lainchu Chroniclings. However, the phrase Chris had used appears to be a mangling of the phrase "a little bird told me", which is used to indicate that a piece of secret information had been received from an undisclosed source, implying that Anaxis was secretly telling him to make both of these lists. Because of this, coupled with Anaxis' recent banning from the Place server, and Chris's dissatisfaction with this decision, the Watchmen became worried that they were losing Chris's interest, and that Anaxis could be manipulating Chris.

A Future in Motion
After Chris's question to Anaxis and Anaxis' discovering of the Watchmen's enabling, Anaxis, knowing that the question indicated skepticism from Chris following the enabling from the Watchmen, decided to make an exception to his rule on not enabling Chris and gave him new lore:
Yes, I still have the connection.
