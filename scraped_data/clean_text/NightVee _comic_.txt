NightVee is a fan spinoff comic of Chris's magnum opus created by MKRNightVee in 2018. MKR posted pages of the comic on her tumblr and Twitter.
When Chris was in contact with MKR and her associates, he implemented many bits of lore from the comic, as well as some of MKR's other ideas, into his own,[1] as well as creating many cards based around the comic and its world for his TSSSF expansion series Nightvee & the Warriors For Andromeda.
Chris has also given his input on the comic on several occasions, and MKR made changes to it accordingly.[2]
