Title: February 2008
URL: https://sonichu.com/cwcki/February_2008
Categories: Category, Chronology
================================================================================

February was the second month of 2008. It was even less eventful than the previous month. Chris continued updating his Facebook status, obviously feeling lonely. Other than playing video games and doing this it's likely he did little else this month. That said, the following month would be far more eventful, to say the least.
Events
2 February - Chris updates his Facebook status to say that he will be going to the early service at his church.
7 February - Chris announces on Facebook that he will be going to the Game Place this evening.
12 February - Chris feels the need to change his Facebook status to say that he is at home.
24 February - Chris's 26th birthday.
25 February - Someone leaves Chris a brief, strange comment on MySpace.
26 February - Someone named "vanessa" on MySpace asks Chris why he's so lonely.
