================================================================================

Nintendo employee reveals details of Chris's Nintendo transactions is a CWCki Forums thread created by Null on 12 January 2015.[1] In it, he posted a message allegedly from a
Nintendo employee leaking the status of Chris's game account. As the post shows, Chris's irresponsible spending habits from the PlayStation Network quickly carried over to the Wii, Wii U and 3DS; among the titles Chris purchased for the platforms, the post states he purchased nearly all of the Virtual Console titles for 3DS, a whole bunch of shovelware, numerous redundant titles (including two sets of the same three Pikmin videos, one 3D and one 2D), and - most interestingly - both Sonic Boom titles for the 3DS and Wii U, despite his intense hatred of the franchise.
The following day, Chris made a Facebook response to the post, claiming that the Sonic Boom games were a gift from Catherine and that he only registered the 3DS game for Club Nintendo points.
