"I Gotta Make My Move" is the fourth song from Christian and the Hedgehog Boys COMEBACK!. It is Chris's version of the Rod Stewart song "Da Ya Think I'm Sexy?".
Chris's cover is about taking a boyfriend-free girl to his "apartment" for hanky-panky. He also encourages sexual harassment of women in the chorus by saying if you "need" a woman, feel free to touch her without her permission.
