Notable events
5 July - Chris and Flutter are spotted at a Walmart customer service line and at the parking lot.[1]
12 July - Chris and Flutter are spotted again at the same Walmart location.
13 July - Chris finishes drawing a Persona 4 commission.[2]
14 July
Chris tweets about not being able to find content he paid for in Pokémon GO.[3][4] This is likely because he had not finished prior content first to unlock it.
Chris joins a Minecraft realm.[5]
15 July - The price of Sonichu Amiibo are lowered.[6]
16 July - Chris and Flutter are spotted driving towards Charlottesville.[7]
18 July - Chris finishes drawing an Azumanga Daioh commission.[8]
27 July - Chris records Minecraft Defeating an Ender Dragon and Minecraft Exploring The End Realm.
30 July - Chris joins the itemLabel Discord server. Chris would be noticed missing from the server on 28 January 2025, though the exact date of his absence is unknown.
