Very First Media Share Suggestion Live Stream
"I support Israel! Palestine[...] they have more... demons and devils within them"
Chris intended for this stream to allow viewers to share various media clips with him.
However, he was plagued with technical issues ranging from being unable to play the clips, to having the audio cut out on him, and after failing to get it to work for about 15 minutes, he gave up and switched to the regular Q&A format.
About halfway through the stream he was finally able to get the clips to work, although the audio only played for him, not the viewers.
