Highlights
1 November
Minecraft Exploring The End Realm. is published.
Chris's car is photographed at a Wendy's in Lynchburg.[1]
2 November - Chris posts more Kamala Harris drawings to Twitter.[2]
4 November
Chris reminds everyone that stealing or defacing the Kamala Harris signs at 14 Branchland Court is illegal.[3]
Chris announces a livestream for 5 November on the election results.[4]
5 November
Minecraft God Dialogue Read is published.
The election of the President of the United States of America occurs. Chris records Jesus Christ Chan Reacting to Presidential Elections 2024, a livestream reacting to the election results, although he stops before the winner is declared. Later, Kamala Harris, who Chris had voted for,[2][5] is defeated by Donald Trump.
6 November
Chris makes a post on Bluesky claiming he is "further making the move" to the platform.[6]
OfficialCWCmart is updated with multiple artworks: the fifth part to Act 4 of Chaos Crystal Control, "Portal Workings" from the 2021 Lost Commissions, and "I Had Returned Home" from OfficialCWCmart Jail Art.
7 November - Chris blocks Caitlyn Jenner's Twitter account over the latter's support of Pmurt.[7]
9 November - Chris makes his "first insightful post" on Bluesky[8] and announces his plan to switch to the platform in a Twitter post.[9]
11 November
Chris announces new "Cwclight exclusive" Rosechu medallions for sale,[10] which are also listed on OfficialCWCmart not long after.
Chris posts Blake Sonichu and A few previously unanswered questions to Cwclight.
16 November - Chris posts Ultra Sonichu to Cwclight.
21 November
Chris removes the "Blue Heart" from "CPU Blue Heart" to add "On ðŸ¦‹ BSky" to the end of his profile name on Twitter and also adds a link to his Bluesky in his bio.
Chris enters a competition for a chance to have his OC Nightstar turned into a plush product sold by Youtooz.[11]
23 November - Chris records Jesus Christ Chan - Shadow Generations Speed Run, a livestream. A comment made by Chris in response to a ween asking him about children stirred up speculation about whether Chris was having or planning to have a child soon.
25 November - Chris reveals that his Lynchburg P.O. box had been "closed due to a miscommunication," and says that mail should be sent to 14 Branchland Court instead.[12]
26 November - Chris begins a commissioned drawing of Persona 5 characters for Ren Ryghts.[13]
27 November
Chris puts an end to the viral false pregnancy rumor that had spread online after his recent stream by making a statement.[14]
The second version of Light Powered Cwclight Reviews - Bumba Lumba is set to private. A third version is published to Chris's YouTube channel again with a re-recorded segment featuring custom box art for Bumba Lumba.
28 November
Thanksgiving.
Son-Chu Review Skit Redux is published to Chris's YouTube channel.
29 November - Chris mentions on Bluesky that he needs $11,000 for dental implants.[15]
