Joshua Conner Moon, better known by his online alias Null, is the current owner of the controversial gossip forum Kiwi Farms (formerly the CWCki Forums). He occasionally contacted Chris between 2013-2021 offering some forms of help, although held conflicts of interest stemming from his forum ownership that resulted in carpetbagging.
During the spring of 2018, Null began acting as one of Chris's volunteer advisors, part of a group dubbed the Guard Dogs. Null attempted to report the Idea Guys to law enforcement, filtered Chris's email and set up another to weed out ween messages from legitimate business dealings,[2] as well as offering advice, such as warning Chris not to invade people's personal space at other conventions after Chris's behavior had gotten him kicked out of TooManyGames. The involvement lasted a few months before Null grew tired of it and cut ties, but Chris reconciled with him in 2020. Null continued acting as manager for Chris, such as attempting to fundraise a trip to Everfree Northwest.
In the wake of the July 2021 incest allegations against Chris, Null was snooping through Chris's email and noticed an alert from Chris's bank that he had received money from his mother Barbara's bank account. Null asked Chris about it, assuming it a violation of the Emergency Protective Order. Chris replied with misleading answers, and Null chose to cut ties with Chris again. Chris felt betrayed that Null had leaked their conversation and now sees Null as a Judas Iscariot-like figure once his Messiah complex reached new heights, since he now believes himself to be Jesus reincarnated.

Ownership of CWCki Forums/Kiwi Farms
Main articles: CWCki Forums and Kiwi Farms
‚Äú

Null, on Chris's current-day trolls.[3]
The CWCki Forums was a forum created by Champthom to serve as a Chris-focused message board; he later passed ownership to Null. In February 2015, Null consolidated and rebranded it into the Kiwi Farms, shifting focus away from just Chris as the board expanded its horizons to other potential lolcows.
Null has often referred to Chris as a "dead cow" and largely shifts his focus to moderating the other boards.[4] As of 2022, his ill-advised antagonism of certain cows (such as non-binary video game developer Near/Byuu and transgender Twitch streamer Keffals) and mismanagement of the site has lead to frequent downtime and deplatforming; this has left Kiwi Farms as a less reliable resource for Chris information and discussion.
1776 Hosting
In 2018, Null launched a hosting service that hosted his Kiwi Farms forum. From 2018-2022, Marvin, the CWCki's owner, had the CWCki on 1776's service. As the result of a wave of DDoS attacks against Kiwi Farms in late summer 2022 which rendered 1776 Hosting unresponsive, Marvin opted to migrate the CWCki to another host in October 2022.

After the 2014 house fire, Null sympathized, as he himself had suffered a house fire in his youth. After Sockness's influence over Chris ended, Chris viewed Null as an ally once again.
During his phase of being possessed by Sonichu, Chris would send a series of messages to Null, spouting bizarre lore about Jacob Sockness. Null responded by first complimenting Chris on the joy he's brought to many people and said that he was open to help him if he needed something. But continued on to say that he refused to role-play or talk with him about things that aren't real. Chris responded somewhat angrily, saying that he really was possessed by Sonichu and continued rambling about more lore until Null finally told him he wasn't interested on giving more attention to Jacob Sockness.
Null joined The Place Discord server where he sometimes chatted with Chris or spoke with server members about him.
In September 2020, Null tried to assist Chris with catching up on unfulfilled customer orders (which Chris took advantage of in a talk with a customer, telling the customer to talk to Null about a delay in shipping instead).[23] In November 2020, Null assisted Chris with arranging potential interviews.[24] In February 2021, Null asked Chris for permission to use his image in the creation and sale of coins for a Kiwi Farms merch run, also offering compensation for using his likeness.[25] Chris agreed to this.[26]
Null has stated that he had a plan for assisting Chris with housing after his mother passes away.[27]
Chris was apparently under the impression Null had ties to organized crime. According to Null, at some point, Chris tried asking him to kill someone.[28] According to Kyle, in February 2021, Chris wanted a hooker and asked Kyle to ask Null to get him one.[29]
In June 2021, Null announced that Chris would attend Everfree Northwest, a My Little Pony convention to be held on 13 August 2021. He mentioned vetoing Chris's plan to drive there and recommended taking a flight, saying he would help Chris with financial arrangements.[30] On 9 July 2021, Null created a GoFundMe campaign to raise money for the trip. The GoFundMe met its $1000 goal 6 hours after going live. Throughout the majority of July 2021, Null helped Chris by continuing to organize and update the GoFundMe campaign.
