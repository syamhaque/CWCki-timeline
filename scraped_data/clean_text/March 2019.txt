Chris in March 2019
March was the third month of 2019. Chris uploaded a video of himself unboxing a Nintendo Switch that was sent by Sarah and Steve, and decided to go ghost hunting with Copitz. Aside from this, Chris also uploaded a video of himself entering an abandoned Sanitarium in order to leave behind a "Goodwill Offering" this month.

Highlights
2 March - Chris attempts to contact Elon Musk because a joke he told on The Big Bang Theory was similar to Chris' beliefs about dimensions.[citation needed]
4 March
Chris writes the letter that he would later leave in DeJarnette.[1]
Chris expresses his displeasure to Sonic's design for the upcoming live action movie, paying special attention to the blarms.[citation needed]
7 March
Chris uploads Very Cool Birthday Present! :), revealing that Sarah and Steve sent him a Nintendo Switch for his birthday.
11 March - Chris announces that one of his husbands, Mewtwo, is ill from a brain virus. [citation needed]
12 March
Chris checks in with the Therapeutic Docket court diversion program,[2] having been penalized with an extra check-in due to missing required weekly meetings with the Boost support group.[3]
Chris lists a pair of his customized sneakers for sale on eBay for $200. Jacob Sockness buys it later that day.[citation needed]
13 March - A Wild Chris-Chan Attempts Human Interaction, an eyewitness video of Chris shopping at a GameStop is uploaded. Chris tweets about Zak Kayes, a pedophile who he deems to be safe because of the confirmation from his OC.[citation needed]
14 March - Chris demands that his house, the Sonichu Headquarters, be made into a Pokéstop for Pokémon Go.[citation needed]
17 March - Sonichu's 19th anniversary; Chris tweets how he almost forgot that fact.[citation needed]
18 March - Chris tweets about Sonichu and Rosechu anatomy.[citation needed]
22 March - Chris goes ghost hunting with Copitz; their exploits were posted the next day.
26 March - Chris makes another court appearance checking in with the Therapeutic Docket.[2] Joseph Draft also films footage for his attempted documentary film Chris Chan VS. The Internet on this and the surrounding days.[4]
30 March - Chris uploads Goodwill Offering and Kindest Wishes, where he pays respect to those abused at the DeJarnette Sanitarium by leaving a Sonichu drawing.
