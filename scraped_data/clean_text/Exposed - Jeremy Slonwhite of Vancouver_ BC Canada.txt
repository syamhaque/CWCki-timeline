Exposed - Jeremy Slonwhite of Vancouver, BC Canada is a video Chris uploaded on 12 August 2011. Chris uses this video to expose information about Thorg and to show that he is very serious in releasing his information. Instead, the video confirmed that Chris had no real leverage against his trolls, as anyone with cursory knowledge of the internet recognized that the supposed picture of Thorg was really the template image for the Butthurt Dweller meme. This is not the first time Chris thought one of his tormentors was the source of an image macro template. It was quickly removed as hate speech. The new Pedo-glasses make their first appearance on video.
