BronyMate is an online dating website for bronies. Chris made an account on this site on 27 August 2017.[1]
The account, using the name of Chris's ponysona, was first discovered by Kiwi Farms users after Chris replied to a tweet by BronyMate on Twitter. The aforementioned tweet said "re-tweet or like if you would hug Princess Celestia" to which Chris responded, "I certainly would Not Block her, just to keep her following haters out. I still would want the Love and Support from the One Hero, always."
