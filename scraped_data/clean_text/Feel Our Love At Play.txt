"Feel Our Love At Play" is Christian and the Hedgehog Boys's version of Berlin's "Take My Breath Away" (a.k.a the romantic theme from Top Gun), from Christian and the Hedgehog Boys COMEBACK!. The song is about how love is no more than a "game" to be "played."
While singing, Chris makes three false starts, indicating that he has no sense of timing. He apparently doesn't care if he messes up and looks like more of an idiot to Ivy.
