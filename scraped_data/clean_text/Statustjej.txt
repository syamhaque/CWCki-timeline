Background
According to Statustjej, they have followed Chris and his misadventures since at least early 2009. Statusthej then went out of their way to download the vast majority of content related to Chris, seemingly up to before the death of Chris's father.
Statustjej saved all of this content on a hard drive, letting it sit for over a decade. While searching for the "Creepy Smile" video in said hard drive, they uploaded the video on YouTube, and have decided to dig through the hard drive to find more videos to post on Wayback Machine's Internet Archive.
