JCWCSP First Minecraft TRICKY TRIALS is a let's play video uploaded by Chris on 14 September 2024. In this video, Chris and several of his new friends, T4ll3n47, CookSUCKS123, and PMDGrovyle explore a trial chambers in Minecraft, added in the Tricky Trials update in 13 June 2024. This is also the first video where the trend of black bars appearing at the top and bottom of the screen begins, which is likely an attempt by Chris's editor to hide the date and time of the original recordings.
