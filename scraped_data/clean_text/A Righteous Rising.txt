A Righteous Rising is an expansion pack of Chris's custom TSSSF cards, featuring art and lore from R. Righteous, credited to Christine's Witness.

Description
Henshin a Go-Go when R. Righteous and his crew ally with the CPUs and She-Ra!
In what one would consider a crazy twist, the CPUs receive new allies from one in their fandom with power Kamen allies and abilities. Now with these heroes and their tech, you can help bring balance on the Shipping Grid.
TSSSS [sic] Core Deck or Sonichu Deck are required for playing the game with this Expansion Pack. This pack contains 32 cards. Card Sleeves are not included.

$19.00[1]
