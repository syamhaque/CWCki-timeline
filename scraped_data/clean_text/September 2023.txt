Title: September 2023
URL: https://sonichu.com/cwcki/September_2023
Categories: Chronology, 2023
================================================================================

September was the ninth month of 2023. As with the past few previous months, Chris was sighted in public numerous times, however, he was also notably first seen being accompanied by an unknown woman as well. Chris also proclaimed his plans to fully come back to the internet in DMs with Helena Fiorenza this month, which he later fulfilled in October 2023.
Notable events
Chris in September 2023.
1 September - Chris is spotted online on the XBox.[1]
3 September - Helena Fiorenza leaks a DM from Chris in which he stated that he will come back to the internet.[2]
4 September - Chris's car is sighted at 14 Branchland Court.[3] Later that day, Chris was sighted at a Food Lion while accompanied by an unknown woman.
6 September - The twelfth commemoration of Bob's passing.
12 September - Chris's incest case is updated with miscellaneous court information.[4]
16 September - Chris is spotted at a Walmart in Bedford, VA.[5]
20 September - Chris is spotted via CCTV footage at a CVS Pharmacy in Lynchburg, VA. Flutter, the lady who had been accompanying him on his store trips appears to be hugged or even kissed by Chris.
21 September - Otaku King claims that Chris is "at a home safe. Just know chris getting the right help needed and won't go through the same BS like before."
27 September - In Animal Crossing: New Horizons, Chris doodles a Sonichu sketch, signing it as J.C.W.C.S.P (Jesus Christine Weston Chandler Sonichu Prime).[6]
30 September - The edited clip of Anthony Logatto screaming "JULAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAY" that had been uploaded to Chris's YouTube account after his arrest is taken down,[7] probably by Chris himself after having regained access to the account.
