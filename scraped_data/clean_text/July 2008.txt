July was the seventh month of 2008. Chris apologized to Michael Snyder to no avail. Megan's birthday passes with no incident.
Events
12 July - In an e-mail to a Game Place friend, Chris says that he has apologized to Michael Snyder for the behavior that got him banned.
23 July - Megan's birthday. It's unknown if Chris managed to give her his present.
