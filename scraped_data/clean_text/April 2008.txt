Title: April 2008

Categories: Category, Chronology
================================================================================

On 4 April 2008, Chris gets a one month ban from The GAMe PLACe.
April was the fourth month of 2008. It was very quiet compared to the tumultuous last month, but it saw Megan sending her final e-mails to Chris and officially cutting all ties with him. Chris also landed himself with a ban from The GAMe PLACe, but luckily for him it was only for 2 weeks.
Events
4 April - Chris gets banned from the GAMe PLACe by Michael Snyder for two weeks, on the grounds that he had been going into the back of the store unauthorized. The actual reason was that Chris was scaring away customers with his behavior.[1]
18 April
Chris's ban from the GAMe PLACe expires, and he returns. Megan doesn't want to talk to him.
In an email, Chris begs Megan to renew their friendship.
21 April - Megan firmly states that she can't trust Chris. This ends their email correspondence.
