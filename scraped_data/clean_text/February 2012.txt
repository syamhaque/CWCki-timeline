Title: February 2012
URL: https://sonichu.com/cwcki/February_2012
Categories: Category, Chronology
================================================================================

February was the second month of 2012. It saw Chris's thirtieth birthday, a slew of unflattering videos made for Jackie, and a look into how he lives his life in a world full of Jews and locust infestations via his newly-public Facebook account.
Chris is not amused.
Chris throws together some art about dating education.
Events
1 February - Chris posts on Facebook that he is lonely and getting harassed by jerkops at Charlottesville Fashion Square.
2 February - Chris posts on Facebook that he is experiencing heartaches.
3 February - Chris tells Kim he is feeling better, and that his Lego minifig is sleeping with Olivia.
4 February - Chris writes on his Facebook account details about playing "hide and seek" with the jerkops and manajerk at Fashion Square Mall.
5 February
Chris is spotted in a Goodwill.
Chris publishes new photos on his Facebook account and announces that he will not accept friend requests from people he has not met in person.
Chris tells his Facebook friends that he recently saw Napoleon Dynamite since he remembered that the "homo" Andy Dick wasn't in it.
7 February - Chris cries about missing high school.
8 February - Jackie creates a Facebook profile, and Chris is very happy about reconnecting with her.
9 February - Chris posts on Facebook that he hopes he and Jackie can get back together again.
10 February - Chris post a picture of Lego avatars of himself and Jackie.
11 February
Chris expresses doubt that Jackie wants to renew a relationship with him.
Chris changes his voicemail greeting...again.
12 February - Chris tells his Facebook friends that they should send him birthday greetings, and that they are welcome to visit his yard on the day.
13 February - Chris flips off a jerkop, angering his mother.
17 February - Chris' plans for a charitable lemonade stand are discovered.
18 February - Chris suddenly realizes that Megan Schroeder was the "seed" behind all his trolling.
20 February - Chris doxxes Megan to his Facebook friends and tell them to bring her in for questioning by Rob Bell.
22 February - The Playstation Vita is released. Chris acquires one within two days.
23 February - Chris posts an image of his ModNation Racers version of Sonichu.
24 February
Chris's drivers' license expires.
Chris turns thirty! Happy Christian Love Day!
TheCWCvilleLibrary releases a grand number of Tomgirl videos aimed at Jackie, who is apparently back in contact with Chris: Cakefarts, Chris and Jackie's Date‎, You're Always Pretty (a wild Snorlax appears!), Little Tomboy Blue‎, Who's Your Brony?, Forgot the Glitter‎, The Red-Maned Deer, Teenage Dream and Cheerleader.
27 February
Chris opens a new YouTube account, CwcvilleGuardian.
Chris expresses anger at Megan and the trolls on Facebook.
28 February - Chris again says that he is furious at Megan.
