King of the Hill Season 14
7 August
9:10 PM
I've just finished watching #KingoftheHill Season 14, and I've already watched the entire first 13 seasons beforehand over the years.
Personally, I feel it is great quality. They kept it in line with the older seasons, so lots of continuity. And the jokes were spot on.
[Posts GIF of Connie Souphanousinphone laughing.]
Although, I was thrown for a loop with Hank and Peggy being in Saudi Arabia for a few years. Still wrapping my head around that. But, I am glad to see most everyone in a later year. Even Bobby ended up a successful chef and restaurant owner. German and Japanese; the combination was quite unique.
I liked the renewed vibe between Bobby and Connie.
Another great part was when they spliced in scenes from the older episodes in flashbacks. Although, I wish they had used the original footage from when Good Hank was born for that flashback.
If I have any complaints about it, it would be the minor detail in some scenes where Peggy's forehead looked big due to the misplacement of her hair in the front. I look forward to Season 15.
Also, where are Luanne and Lucky?
