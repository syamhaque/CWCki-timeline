Title: March 2022

================================================================================

The Return to Sender stamp from Spamton's last letter, confirming that Chris has been transferred to a private location.
March was the third month of 2022. Chris was moved from Central Virginia Regional Jail at the end of February; he spent March 2022 in Western State Hospital.
Notable events
1 March - Spamton shares a letter from Chris dated 30 January and attached prayer dated 13 January.
2 March - Bizarre Bazaar shares a letter from Chris dated 30 January and attached prayer dated 13 January.
15 March - Spamton's last letter to Chris is returned to its sender, confirming without a doubt that Chris has been transferred to a private location.[1]
19 March - TheGamerFromMars releases the first episode of his Chris-Chan documentary that features snippets of a previously-unseen tour of 14 Branchland Court and an interview with Chris over a year before his arrest.
26 March - TheGamerFromMars releases the second episode of a Chris-Chan documentary, featuring footage of Chris playing Twilight Sparkle's Secret Shipfic Folder.
