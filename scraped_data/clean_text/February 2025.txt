Notable events
1 February - Chris posts The Autobots of Cwcville to Cwclight.
4 February - Chris says something new is coming to his stores, posting a teaser image of the talking Sonichu and Rosechu plushes.[1]
6 February - Talking Sonichu and Rosechu plushes are listed on OfficialCWCmart, as well as a commissions listing for custom medallions.
10 February - Caden is charged with speeding in Amherst County. Notably, his address is listed as Big Island, VA.[2]
14 February - Chris and Flutter are spotted together at a pet store.[3]

Barbara is photographed at 14 Branchland Court; a ween claims that she said, "“I have a son. It’s his birthday today."[4][5]
25 February - TheGamerFromMars posts a teaser image with Chris to Twitter, adding "Soon."
