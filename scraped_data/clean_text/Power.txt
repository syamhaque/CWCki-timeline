Power is a zipfile published by The Miscreants containing three videos in which Chris performs various dubious feats of strength. The .zip includes the three videos, an audio file edited from "I Got a Fish" and a "special thanks" document. Chris gave these files to Joshua Martinez to give to Vanessa Hudgens to meet her request for a "test of strength" as part of her audition process. The zipfile was leaked along with CWCFlyingElephants on 27 April 2009.
The original zipfile had long been considered lost media since the shutdown of the original Megaupload, on which it had been hosted. In October 2025, however, Statustjej confirmed the special thanks file's contents in the comments of their Chris Chan creepy smile reupload, before uploading the original zipfile (along with other Chris content) to the Internet Archive on 12 November.
Since Power's release, videos of Chris exercising (or attempting exercise) have been dubbed "Power Videos".
