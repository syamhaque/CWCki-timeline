Highlights
2 January - A day late, Chris wishes his audience a happy new year.
4 January
Chris has an inability to feel like drawing and writing the stories because Lulu had called him out on Sonichu being fan-fiction back in 2015. He also whines more about financial troubles and feeling lost; however, instead of applying for a job, he begs for money.
Chris wants to discourage anyone from portraying Donald Trump's face.
Chris tweets the link for his puzzler to Trump's Twitter account, with the death threat still intact.
Chris comments on twelve pages from IDW's Love is Love comic (an anthology dedicated to victims of the 2016 Pulse Nightclub shooting).
8 January - Chris would like to wear a vagina necklace.
9 January
Chris is angry that he was barred from donating blood because he is transgender.
For the second time, Chris tweets the link for his puzzler to Trump's Twitter account, with the death threat still intact.
10 January - Images from Ancestry.com of documents pertaining to the Chandler Family Tree are released to Kiwi Farms by one of Chris's distant cousins.
19 January - Text message emerges revealing that Chris doesn't really care about the new Nintendo Switch console, stating how he is preoccupied.
20 January
Donald Trump takes office, succeeding Barack Obama. Chris takes to YouTube, Facebook and Twitter to complain.
A photo of Chris in Tomgirl mode is briefly featured in a lecture delivered by Breitbart News editor Milo Yiannopoulos at the University of Washington.
22 January - Chris types out an open letter to Barron Trump, in which he urges Barron not to become a "Super Stereotypical, Spiteful and Egotistical Male" like his father.
