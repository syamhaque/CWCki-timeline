Chris Chan Conservation Collective is a Discord server. It is known for being the server in which Fiona chatted with other users in regards to the Incest Call that Bella had shared with her. It also details the creation of the second leak of the Incest Call which would follow several hours after the first. Server member GunLoader420 leaked the Incest Call onto YouTube and Reddit.
Fiona's messages will be represented in magenta and various other users in the chat will be represented with other colors.
