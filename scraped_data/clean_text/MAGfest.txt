MAGfest (Music And Gaming Festival) is a video game and music convention held annually in National Harbor, Maryland, just outside of Washington, D.C. Chris attended in 2018.

2011
Chris wanted to go during 2011, thinking he could hook up with a friend's cousin while there. However, he failed to budget money and could not afford a room.[1] When he told his parents of the dilemma, and his potential solution of sleeping in the hotel lobby or car, they forbade him from going, citing cold weather and the high crime rate, and threatened to lock him out of the house if he went. Chris tried to manipulate them with an "Autistic Treatment" (ignoring them during a family dinner) at Country Cookin', but later at home his mother threatened suicide to manipulate him into feeling guilty enough to cancel his ill-conceived plans. Altogether, Chris lost $40 from preregistration.[2]
2018
Schedule for the CWC panel. Yes, really.[3]
Chris' interest in going during 2018 was apparently piqued in December 2017 when Marvin informed him that there would be a CWC-themed panel.[4] On arrival, Chris was met by a small group of fans who ended up cheering him on, as well as recording a selection of his trademark antics.
Several people sought him out for photos.
