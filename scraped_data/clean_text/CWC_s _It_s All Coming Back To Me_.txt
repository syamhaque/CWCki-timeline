Released 24 April 2009, CWC's "It's All Coming Back To Me" is Chris's second music video. It depicts Christian enacting his ideal proposal, and is followed by another comic about his ideal wedding to Ivy. He provides vocals over the song "It's All Coming Back to Me Now" by Celine Dion.
