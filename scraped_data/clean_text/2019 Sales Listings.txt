For once, Chris chose to sell something of interest and value to collectors, although it seems that he has significantly overpriced the coins - for example, in ordinary circulated condition, a 1939 Mercury dime normally only costs a maximum of $3, with higher prices being reserved for examples in better condition. Contrary to Chris's descriptions of the items in their titles, the coins have not been graded by a numismatic authority (in coin collection, such ungraded coins are considered 'raw') making their actual condition unverified.
