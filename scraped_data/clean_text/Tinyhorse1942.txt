tinyhorse1942, aka TinyHorse, is a Twitter account suspected to be a sock used by Chris for evading blocks on his main account. The account was created in March 2021. Posts strongly resembling Chris's writing style appeared in January 2024. The account name is derived from Caden Peck's online handle tinyhorse01, possibly meaning Caden gave the account to Chris. It is used mainly to follow several of the current and previous TF2 Analysts, ProjectSNT, Nowacking and Gleamy Dreams, all of whom have blocked Chris's main account on Twitter, as well as several Finnish learning platforms. Nowadays, it has devolved into another channel of the typical anti-G5 protests.
