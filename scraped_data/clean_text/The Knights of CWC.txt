The Knights of CWC server.
The Knights of CWC was a splinter group of the Watchmen. Chris was in their Discord server from 25 June 2021[1] until his August 2021 arrest for incest. It was founded by The WCT following his fallout with Naught, Kyle and MKR over Naught's doxing of WCT leading to bad blood between them. WCT was unsure of calling his group by the Watchmen moniker. He also stated he wanted the group be kept secret as the earlier iteration of the Watchmen once had been.[2] He modeled the structure of the Knights server after The Place.[3]
The Knights aimed to take a less aggressive approach than The Place when it came to other enablers,[4] planning only to intervene in cases of malicious intentions.[4][5] such as Jacob Sockness's plot to attend Everfree Northwest in disguise to meet with Chris.[6]
