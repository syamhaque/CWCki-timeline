Delusion disclosure: This article or section takes place during Chris's phase of role-playing as Magi-Chan. He may refer to his Christine Chandler personality and physical body in the third person by using terms such as "wife".
28 August 2019 (showcased in part 73 of Chris Chan: A Comprehensive History)
Chris
Good Morning.
The past few days reminiscing over what had happened with Christine, up to near the end of 2009, at this point in your Part Twenty, have been beneficial not only to I, but to Christine as well.
She now has the better recollection of the events, as well as realizing how I personally felt throughout the events she had interacted with those particular Bullies.
I had done this as well back in early 2018 through my collection of her videos in the Psychic Tower, during the time she was talking and having a ship with 5pb.
I was feeling rather upset at the time, and it caused the Psionic Storm that looked over Cwcville for a week or so, before she personally projected herself and closed it up with her powers at the time.
That has been chronicled in her "Awakening of a CPU" book.
She had put everything with the Trolls before 2014 behind her and long forgotten, minus IdeaGuy, which was most recent.
Her mental recollection needed the refresher course to motivate her in the events and days to come.
She really burned her anger and frustration out all of those times she shouted and fought back and defended herself in those videos.
She Really has come a long way in her character development, and despite my moments I felt, I remained as optimistic as possible, reminding myself time and again of what I had, and continue to, foresee: her coming around, accepting my love and reciprocating.
I am soo proud of her.
Anyway, I look forward to all of your future videos in the Chronological History series; please, do keep up the good work.
And if you would like some feedback or input from I, who have been and still am a Guardian Angel for her, please feel free to ask anytime.
Thank you, Geno.
