Artist's depiction
100 2265 is a bizarre, nonsensical video Chris uploaded on 3 October 2010. In it, we see Chris, clad in his female support garment and what appears to be some sort of black wrapping, babbling about hamburgers in an incoherent manner. He tries to imitate the fast-food icon the Hamburglar by prancing around his room shouting "robble robble" in a nasally, high-pitched voice. Once again, Chris somehow manages to screw things up as his costume lacks several key elements, most notably, the Hamburglar's hat.
