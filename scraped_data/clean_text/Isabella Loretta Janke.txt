Isabella "Bella" Loretta Janke, (born circa 2000s)[note 10] also known as Kelly Osborn and dozens of other pseudonyms, is a foot fungus-ridden[18] ween and self-described "lonely 4chan dweller",[12] known for recording conversations initiated by Chris in which he bragged about his incest with his mother.
In the fall of 2020, Bella was hired by the Praetor group to create the Chris Chan Sonichu Intro animation for Chris. Chris began contact with her afterwards.
While Bella was friendly towards Chris in conversations,[19] behind his back she A-Logged him on Kiwi Farms[8] (saying that she tried to tell the trolls to get him to cut his cock off and that she thought Chris "should‚Äôve been killed in some freak gender reassignment surgery accident by now") and her private Chess Club Discord server[20] (in which Allen and Bella had made what Allen later claimed to be edgy and sarcastic jokes about a plan to get Chris to kill himself).[1] Bella's comments led to much speculation and rumors, though no proof has been found that Bella had been serious with her A-logging remarks.
In July 2021, Chris began telling selected trusted individuals - Bella, The WCT, Null, Don Lashomb, Spunky, and his physician[21][22] - that he had begun having sexual relations with an older woman. At the time, Chris was using euphemisms such as "lady friend" to mislead them and disguise the true nature of his actions.
On the night of 27 July 2021 EST,[23] Chris trusted Bella to such a degree that he decided to brag that his sexual partner was his own mother to her during a video call conversation with her. Bella covertly and partially recorded the conversation[24] and shared it with the Chess Club Discord server and Fiona. Chris also sent Incest Texts describing the incest further. Bella planned on how to release the information publicly, wanting to gather evidence and get more information from Chris from another angle to preserve her anonymity.[25] However, Fiona shared audio from the call with the Chris Chan Conservation Collective Discord server,[26] and around the early morning of 30 July, the first of multiple leaks of audio from the call was leaked to YouTube and Reddit by Chris Chan Conservation Collective server member EllDudeRocks, followed by the texts being leaked to Reddit by Fiona. Longer (though non-essential) cuts of the call were released by Bella and her Chess Club friend over the next few days. Ultimately Chris would be arrested and charged with incest after concerned viewers called police for a welfare check after the initial 30 July leaks.
In Bella's attempts to preserve her own anonymity, she tried to claim that her friends were either behind the leak[27] or were the person behind her voice in the recording,[28] a move which led to much backlash. She also inexplicably crafted poorly-photoshopped fabricated BDSM texts and manipulated others to post them onto Kiwi Farms, another move which further damaged her credibility.
In the wake of the Incest Call's leak, Bella was doxed by the Kiwi Farms.[29] Out of a combination of her A-Log comments and self-proclaimed plots towards Chris, her edgy behavior, drama over the revelation of Chris having sex with his mother, and general confusion as logs of Bella's chats were leaked, speculation and gossip on Bella's involvement with Chris (led by Kiwi Farms user Spooky Bones) ran rampant, resulting in misinformation being repeated as fact.
After being sent to jail over the incest leaks, Chris began viewing Bella as an enemy, alongside Null and Jacob Sockness, even going as far to falsely claim in many jail letters that he "foresaw" several of her claimed plots and that Bella had faked many of the details that were revealed in the Incest Call. Chris also exploited misinformation created by gossipers, repeating those as part of his narrative.
