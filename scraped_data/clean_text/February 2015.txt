================================================================================

on 1 February 2015, Chris unveils his commission for a noise music album cover.
On 5 February 2015, Chris looks even more old cat lady-like than usual at court.
February was the 2nd month of 2015. This month began with Chris revealing his artwork for an obscure power-noise group's most recent release, followed by a widely-publicized (on Kiwi Farms, nonetheless) documentary about him seeing release on YouTube, and him updating his eBay for the first in a very long time.
On the 5th, Chris had a court hearing for the events of 26 December 2014. It would be continued to 2 April. Notably, from eyewitness accounts given, Chris came in his tomgirl attire, eliciting a response from his public defender.
Events of February 2015
1 February - Chris reveals that had been commissioned to do the album artwork for the newest release from noise music group L'eclipse Nue, Negative.[1]
2 February
The fan-made documentary Christian Weston Chandler is released by Sachumo. The video has over a million views.
Chris makes a triumphant return to eBay.
5 February - Chris's court date following the GameStop incident takes place. The case is set to continue on 2 April 2015.[2]
10 February
Chris makes an update on his medallion business.
Flipnote Studio 3D is finally released in North America.
11 February - Chris announces his excitement over the aforementioned game on Facebook.
13 February - A deluxe edition of Trollsta's Paradise: A True and Honest Tribute to Christian & The Hedgehog Boys is released.
14 February - Valentine's Day. Like previous years, Chris does not have any big plans for the holiday.
24 February - Chris turns 33. To celebrate, the first segments of audio of Chris's date with Catherine are leaked to the Farms.
26 February - More audio of Chris's date with Catherine is released to the Farms.
27 February - Chris shares an article on Facebook about the death of Leonard Nimoy.[3]
28 February - Another 30 minutes of Chris's secretly recorded date with Catie is leaked.
