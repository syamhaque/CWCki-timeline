"I'll Never Leave You, Ivy" is the fifth song off of Christian and the Hedgehog Boys COMEBACK!. It is Chris's version of "Wuthering Heights" by Kate Bush. Said song is notoriously difficult to sing thanks to Kate's high-pitched and fluctuating vocal style (which you can naturally hear in the background while Chris drones away in his signature monotone), so it shouldn't be a surprise to anybody that Chris is unable to retain the spirit of the original song.
Ironically, the title "I'll Never Leave You, Ivy" turned out to be correct, because Ivy dumped Chris before he had a chance to leave her.
