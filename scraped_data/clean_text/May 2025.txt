Notable events
1 May - Chris watches and makes a comment in the live-chat on a YouTube video by ILoveKimPossibleAlot.[1] Chris wrote a post chain on Bluesky about the YouTube video.[2]
2 May - Light Powered Cwclight Reviews - Fireshot is published.
5 May - YouTube takes down Geno Samuel's in-progress Sonichu: The Animated Series after Chris files a copyright claim over its use of his Sonichu 0.[3] Chris later comments on it.[4]
10 May - Chris attends the Festival of Light in Lynchburg and blows at least $45 there,[5] marking his first confirmed day back in America after concluding his fourth trip to Finland.
12 May - Chris posts to Bluesky to talk about drama involving Saberspark and ILoveKimPossibleAlot, and mentioned doing a livestream soon.[6] He also wrote: "I am even expanding into digital drawing, as you all will see soon enough."
16 May - Light Powered Cwclight Reviews - Rollout is published.
17 May
Chris announces that he will being doing a livestream later in the day.[7] He then records Jesus Christ Chan - Sonic 2006 Speed Playthrough Part 3.
Chris took a break from the livestream to call a donor, who then sought information about the Dimensional Merge, where he lives and Flutter.[8]
18 May - Chris is spotted at a Walmart in Lynchburg.[9]
19 May
Light Powered Cwclight Reviews - Medatron is published.
Grounded Deep Response is published.
20 May - Light Powered Cwclight Reviews - Chris-Chan Prime is published.
21 May - Chris posts Dimension Merge supplements and The Book of the Dimension Merge to Cwclight.
23 May - Chris posts Transformers Legacy United Merge Bios and Random Thoughts (will be updated from time to time) to Cwclight.
30 May - Light Powered Cwclight Reviews - S.A.V. (Scoundrel As Vessel) the First‎ is published.
