Gilligan's Island is an American sitcom involving the hijinks of the eponymous Gilligan and his fellow castaways on a desert island, airing on CBS from 1964 to 1967. It is one of the many ancient sitcoms Chris enjoys.
Mentions by Chris
Thurston Howell, III and his wife Lovey make an appearance in Sonichu #8, hinting at the surname of the head of 4-cent_garbage.com, the infamous Jason Kendrick Howell.
In For My Sweetest Ivy, Chris said that he had the entire series of Gilligan's Island on DVD, and that he and Ivy could watch it together.
Chris created a Gilligan's Island quiz on the MyBUZZ! website.
Chris alluded to the episode "The Second Ginger Grant" in a 30 July 2009 email to Katie Bay.
In the 18 October 2010 video Gilligan Be or Not, Chris performed a dreadful rendition of Gilligan's performance of Hamlet in "The Producer".
