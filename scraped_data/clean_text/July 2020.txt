Chris in July 2020
Delusion disclosure: This article or section takes place during Chris's phase of role-playing as Sonichu. He may refer to his Christine Chandler personality and physical body in the third person by using terms such as "mama".
July was the seventh month of 2020. The COVID-19 pandemic continued to ravage the U.S., while Chris remained deep into his bodyswap with Sonichu. Chris also attended an online Google hangout chat with the Watchmen this month.

Highlights
1 July
While roleplaying as Sonichu, Chris indirectly orders Jacob Sockness to back off, having mustered the courage to tell him off for his creepy behavior again.
The Official CWCki Server is created.
2 July - Chris handwrites the Whispering Spell (Restore Magic).
6 July - Wes Iseli, magician and former boyfriend of Sarah Hammer, makes an appearance on the TV series Penn & Teller: Fool Us.
8 July - Chris lists out dozens of supposed trolls on Twitter, urging his personal army to attack them.[citation needed]
14 July - Dimensional Barrier Shatter Spell is handwritten by Chris.
15 July - Chris tweets a creepy, sexually suggestive remark to an artist he has been orbiting and is subsequently blocked by her.
16 July - Chris logs into Kiwi Farms and has a conversation with Null. During it, Chris rambles for paragraphs about his fantasy role-playing, including his belief that he is possessed by Sonichu, lore involving Jacob Sockness, and the Iron Curtain. Null responds that he will not play along with the game.[1]
17 July - Jacob Sockness DMs Chris to promote a ritual.[2]
18 July - Jacob Sockness posts a tweet in which he performs a ritual, which involves using a drop of his blood on a "Magi-Chan Card" and calling himself "Blood Brothers" with Magi-Chan. Chris, fed up, said that the ritual had failed.[citation needed]
20 July
Chris has a Google Hangout chat with the Watchmen.
The first of the known Kai DMs.
30 July - The first of the known Naught DMs.
