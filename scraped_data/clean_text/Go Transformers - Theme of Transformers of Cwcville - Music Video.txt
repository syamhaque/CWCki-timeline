Go Transformers - Theme of Transformers of Cwcville - Music Video is a video uploaded to Chris's YouTube channel on 12 September 2025. It is a self-made music video set to a custom theme song Chris wrote for the CWCville version of the Transformers, which was then converted to a full-on song with the AI site Suno. The video heavily utilizes very rudimentary stop-motion, and is frequently broken up by brief, random skits.
This marks the very first time Chris made a comment in the comment section on his own video.[1]
