14BC in October 2022
October was the tenth month of 2022. The sealing of the case means nothing new was heard from Chris, but a Kiwi Farms member took new footage of 14 Branchland Court.
Meanwhile, with the troubles of the Kiwi Farms continuing in the aftermath the Keffals incident, Marvin moved the CWCki to a new host after four years, allowing it to return online on 22 October 2022.[1]
Notable events
1 October - Barb turns 81, her second birthday in a row without Chris.
5 October - A hearing is scheduled to take place for Portfolio Recovery Associates' filing of garnishment against Chris.[2]
11 October - New footage of 14 Branchland Court is recorded.
15 October - New pictures of 14 Branchland Court released.
20 October - Chris draws "Naitsirhc Failed, Even With a Wig, Glasses, and Moustache"
22 October - The CWCki returns after over two months of downtime.
31 October - Halloween: Chris spent it in jail.[3]
