Notable events
4 April
Chris uploads Light Powered Cwclight Reviews - Wave.
A new photograph of Chris in Finland is posted to Kiwi Farms, marking Chris's fourth known trip to the country.[1]
11 April - The fifth episode of The Chris Chan Conspiracy is released, featuring new footage of TheGamerFromMars interviewing Chris at various locations. This episode is notable for revealing new information about the Jail Saga.
12 April - Chris's uncle, and the widower of Aunt Corrina, Raymond Inge dies at age 89.[2]
16 April - Chris uploads Reviews from Chris Chan Etsy Store April 2025.
17 April - Chris and Flutter are spotted and photographed eating in Finland.[3]
18 April - Chris uploads Light Powered Cwclight Reviews - Chapper.
