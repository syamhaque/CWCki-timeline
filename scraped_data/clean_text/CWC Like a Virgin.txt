"CWC Like a Virgin" is Christian and the Hedgehog Boys's cover (in the loosest sense of the word) of Madonna's "Like a Virgin" (the background audio being sourced from her Confessions Tour live album). It is the second track off of Christian and the Hedgehog Boys COMEBACK!.
the song can also be heard here along with every other song on the album.
