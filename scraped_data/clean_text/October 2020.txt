Delusion disclosure: This article or section takes place during Chris's phase of role-playing as Sonichu. He may refer to his Christine Chandler personality and physical body in the third person by using terms such as "mama".
Chris in October 2020
October was the tenth month of 2020. Throughout the month, Chris continued to roleplay as Sonichu and finally finished watching the TF2 Analysis video series. Chris drove to Pennsylvania to drop off goods to a customer and attempt to make an unsolicited visit to YouTuber emgo316. Chris also recorded an update video about the Dimensional Merge, and was interviewed on the The Create Unknown podcast this month.
Highlights
1 October - Barb turns 79.
2 October
Chris records a reaction video to the fifth part of TF2 Analysis's Red Vs Blue brony cartoon.[1]
Chris informs Ben Saint that he did not curse Donald Trump with COVID-19, and claims that he will not die from the disease.[citation needed]
3 October - An enabler texts Chris wanting to help produce a Chris Chan Bible.[2] Chris also discusses it with the Watchmen.[3]
8 October
Chris drives to Pennsylvania to drops off autographed items for a fan and to attempt a visit to YouTuber emgo316. The visit fails as Chris did not notify Emgo of the trip and tried to search his address online.[4]
Chris officially endorses Democratic Party nominees Joe Biden and Kamala Harris for President and Vice President.[5]
9 October
Chris analyzes a dream involving a golden stone disc, mashing together lore from various sources, to conclude that the Iron Curtain can be breached between 22-23 October.[6]
Chris tweets out the cover artwork for the incomplete Sonichu 17 comic.[citation needed]
13 October
Chris takes several photos posing with a group of people known as Praetor.[citation needed]
The first of the known Bella chats, in which Bella attempts to impersonate a Curiosity employee to Chris.
14 October - Chris discusses Praetor over DMs with Naught.[7]
15 October - Chris prepares a script for a video on the Merge to be filmed the following day. Praetor is invited into The Place and discusses some of their business ideas.[8]
16 October - Chris finishes his script for an upcoming video about the Dimensional Merge, which is to be recorded with the help of Praetor.[9]
18 October - Country Cookin closes all of its locations due to the financial fallout of the COVID-19 pandemic.
19 October - Chris introduces himself to the Sonic World DX Lounge Discord server.
20 October - Chris receives a hundred dollar figure of My Little Pony character Rainbow Dash he had pre-ordered months ago and excitedly posts pictures of it in The Place.[10]
21 October - Chris announces in The Place that "it is happening now," referring to his prediction that a Dimensional Merge event will happen the next night.[11]
22 October - The bead string on Chris's medallion breaks again and he repairs it. He then goes to Burger King to eat a fast food meal which he and Bob used to enjoy. Chris then prepares for a Dimensional Merge related event which he believes will occur at midnight.[12]
23 October - In The Place, Chris describes the ritual he had performed to further break the Iron Curtain, announcing "we did it".[13]
26 October - Chris answers more questions on Discord.[14]
28 October - Chris is interviewed on The Create Unknown Podcast.
30 October - Chris records a reaction video to the sixth and final part of TF2 Analysis's Red Vs Blue brony cartoon.[15]
31 October - Halloween.
