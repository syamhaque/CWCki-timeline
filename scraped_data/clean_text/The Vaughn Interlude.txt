The Vaughn Interlude is the second of the Lainchu Chroniclings, posted on the CWCki by Anaxis on 4 February 2021.
It details behind-the-scenes events regarding the troll Larry Vaughn, and the discussions that occurred about them in The Watchmen's Place during January 2021. Anaxis named this collection The Chroniclings of Chris Chan: The Vaughn Interlude, describing the event as "a cautionary tale." As before with the previous collection of screenshots, Anaxis made color-coded censors and redactions. Messages by Chris (as Sonichu) are color-coded yellow throughout the article, while the Watchmen and their messages are named and color-coded after their respective censor colors.
