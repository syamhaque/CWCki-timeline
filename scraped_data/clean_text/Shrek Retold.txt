Shrek Retold
Information
Released
29 November 2018
Shrek Retold is a crowdfunded shot-for-shot remake of the 2001 film Shrek, released on 29 November 2018. It was organized by 3GI Productions for their annual Shrekfest convention. 200 people contributed their own versions of scenes from the movie. 3GI is a follower of Chris's and reached out to him the year before the release date asking for his participation in "a project".[2] Notably, Bryanfrogboy is also a contributor to the project.
Chris was assigned scene #23 out of 200, in which Shrek is convinced by Lord Farquaad to go on a quest to rescue Princess Fiona so he can have his swamp back. Chris's scene features voice acting and crudely colored stills that were surprisingly animated (though the animation was done by others).[3] His scene is a little over a minute long. The audio on his scene also continually skips numerous times and the video has the characteristic piss filter, indicating that it was filmed on his abused PSEye.
Shrek Retold was a rare instance where Chris was asked to be part of something, Chris did it and completed the task on time, and no drama was started as a result of harassment by weens or Chris himself. This was not the case for the sequel Shrek 2 Retold, from which Chris was axed over his incest scandal.
Shrek Retold has amassed more than 10 million views, making it the most-viewed video in Christory by default.

Chris's drawing
Chris also drew a piece of artwork featuring Sonichu and Shrek which was featured as a raffle item at the August 2019 Shrekfest.[5]
Shrek 2 Retold
Shrek 2 Retold is a sequel to the Shrek Retold project, scheduled to be released in September 2024. While it was in production, Chris had returned and contributed art and voicework. However, due to his subsequent incest scandal, he was removed from the project.[6]
