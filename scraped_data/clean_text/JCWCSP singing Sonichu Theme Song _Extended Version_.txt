JCWCSP singing Sonichu Theme Song (Extended Version) is a video published to Chris's YouTube channel on 24 October 2025. In it, Chris poorly sings a new
Sonichu Theme Song. Most of the lyrics are a combination of the lyrics from Sonichu Zip and orcoe30's Sonichu Cartoon Theme. After singing, Chris shouts "Kra-kow" and the video ends.
The video was already uploaded to YouTube, set as 'private' in the JCWCSP Sings playlist, on 10 October 2025.
