January was the first month of 2021. The year started off pretty quietly, with most of Chris's content this month being tied to a variety of Twitter posts.

Highlights
1 January - Chris believes the Dimensional Merge has hit an apex.[1]
2 January - Miss L and Chris begin texting.[2]
3 January
The Cirrusfire DMs are leaked.
Chris comes into contact with Larry Vaughn.[3]
4 January - Chris shows MKRNightVee a Transformers Funko Pop he had bought and says he thought it to be something she might like and asks confirmation for her address, which she provides.[4]
13 January - Larry Vaughn and Chris text each other, archived in Larry Vaughn DMs.
14-15 January - As a result of being manipulated by Larry Vaughn, Chris DMs multiple Twitter users messages containing bizarre sexual and religious statements, including an Albert Fish quote about a shit-eating fetish.[5][6]
16 January - Null advises Chris not to tweet an acknowledgement that he had sent out Twitter DMs featuring an Albert Fish quote about a shit-eating fetish.[7]
17 January
Chris tweets an acknowledgement that he had sent out Twitter DMs featuring an Albert Fish quote about a shit-eating fetish.[8]
Null scolds Chris for ignoring his advice not to tweet the acknowledgement.[7]
18 January - Chris posts a wall of text explaining his "body-swap" with Sonichu and continued belief in the Dimensional Merge.[9]
20 January - Joe Biden is inaugurated as the 46th President of the United States of America, succeeding Donald Trump.
23 January - Following Chris's request, a series of Discord screenshots entitled The Journey to the Merge is released by a Watchmen member.[10]
24 January - Chris gossips about Larry Vaughn in The Place and announces that he's done with Vaughn.[11]
