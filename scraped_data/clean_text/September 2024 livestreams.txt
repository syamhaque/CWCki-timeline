Highlights
02:07: [Stream begins]
03:54: "He's a mean one, Mr. Trump."
04:44: Chris drinks from Bob's favorite mug.
05:25: Chris gives his thoughts on Haitians in Ohio eating dogs. "I mean, that's their choice and I'm not gonna say one way or the other, though personally... I'm against it." Chris also says he wouldn't eat dog meat long term but might try a sample.
06:35: Chris talks about his day, mentioning his visit back to Ruckersville to sort things out from boxes relating to "future plans", as well as placing the Kamala Harris sign in front of 14 Branchland Court.
08:32: Chris is aware of the Beetlejuice themed Fanta but says "I have not drank Fanta at all... not lately."
10:44: A donor asks Chris if he would go on Fishtank for $10,000. Chris replies "I don't know about Fishtank."
12:58: A donor asks Chris if he ever tried smoking weed. Chris says he has sampled before but hasn't done it in years.
15:00: Kamala Harris and Donald Trump take the stage.
15:15: A donor asks Chris what country he would want to go to besides America. Chris answers London, England.
21:14: Donald Trump calls himself an open book, prompting Chris to laugh.
23:54: "He's lying out of his ass right there."
27:19: Donald Trump says he was going to send Kamala Harris a MAGA hat, prompting Chris to yell "Nobody wants a MAGA hat, Trump! Nobody wants anything MAGA!" Chris also calls Donald Trump a "toxic traitor", but takes it back after thinking he went too far, instead opting to call him a "toxic narcissist."
33:08: Chris claps for Kamala Harris after she talks about incest and abortion.
41:11: A donor asks Chris about his thoughts on abortion, Chris says that he is pro-choice and that Roe v. Wade should be reinstated.
43:30: Chris calls himself a moderate.
48:14: Chris mentions Mar-a-Lago in reference to the FBI search of Mar-a-Lago, which happened in August 2022 while Chris was in jail.
50:58: Chris gives his thoughts on Guilty Gear in response to a donor. "I have not played much of the Guilty Gear series. But- but- but from- from what I've played, the little bit of it, I'd say it's a decent game series. And I've heard some things from WatchMojo as well."
57:13: Chris does an evil laugh.
59:18: Chris makes a loud noise.
1:00:23: Kamala Harris brings up Charlottesville.
1:07:35: Donald Trump will not survive the Dimensional Merge and is going down with Vladimir Putin.
1:09:26: A donor asks Chris if he would fight in a celebrity boxing match. Chris replies "I have no need to fight in a celebrity boxing match."
1:11:21: Donald Trump says "If I were president, Russia would have never ever, I know Putin very well," prompting Chris to laugh, slam his desk, and say "Oh, the irony in that statement just now!"
1:16:00: The presidential debate takes a short break. Chris gets up to leave the room, revealing his lower back.
1:18:18: Chris returns with Pocky sticks.
1:20:42: The presidential debate resumes.
1:41:27: Chris gets asked about Xenoblade. Chris replies "I am aware of the Xenoblade series, but I have not played very much of it at all."
1:48:30: The presidential debate takes a short break. Chris gets up to leave the room, revealing his lower back again.
1:51:45: Chris returns. Chris refuses a donor's request for him to say "Hawk Tuah" because he has a feeling that it is a bad type of meme.
1:53:18: The presidential debate resumes for the final closing statements.
1:56:39: Chris meows.
1:57:53: Chris stops watching the presidential debate livestream and talks briefly before ending the livestream.
