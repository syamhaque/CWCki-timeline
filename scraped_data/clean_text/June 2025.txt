Notable events
2 June - Chris posts "Prophetic Details" to the CWC Blog.
8 June - Chris shares his very own AI chatbot replicating himself.[1]
9 June - Waltah White repeatedly calls Chris's phone,[2] and the calls are recorded and then posted six days later. In the brief phone calls, Chris gets angry at Walt for "betraying" him.
13 June - Chris publishes Light Powered Cwclight Reviews - Decepticlone Crackder to YouTube.
20 June - Chris films the ending skit of Light Powered Cwclight Reviews - Gwenmi Mikoto, it is very likely that the Light Powered Cwclight Reviews videos from C-C 82 (Chris-Chan Origin Form) to Sparky were filmed around this time.
29 June - Chris publishes Discovering Transformers Legacy United Merged Wave 3 to YouTube.
