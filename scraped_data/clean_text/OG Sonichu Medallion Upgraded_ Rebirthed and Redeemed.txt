Chris's brand new medallion, the Mark IV.
OG Sonichu Medallion Upgraded, Rebirthed and Redeemed is a video uploaded to Chris's YouTube channel on 18 September 2025. In it, Chris takes apart his Mark III medallion with his bare hands, cracking the moldy crusty clay into bits. He takes the destroyed pieces of the medallion and infuses it into his brand new one, which he bakes into the clay as seen at the end of the video. According to Chris, this is the "rebirth" of his medallion, since he mixed the old pieces inside it.

Description
As all of you can see and appreciate: I, Jesus Christine Weston Chandler Sonichu, Prime of every single Chris Chan and Christ Chan throughout the Multiverse, have broken up my original, Model-Magic crafted Sonichu Medallion, put the cruciald and some of the other, pieces into clay, and remade my Medallion, renewed, rebirth and redeemed. It was hard on me to break it up, but it was my pleasure to renew and redeem it with my newer energy and stronger powers and abilities.

0:00-0:23: [The TV is playing Invader Zim in the background. The medallion is sitting on its face on Chris's desk, Chris picks it up and flips it around then places it back on the table facing upwards before the video cuts. His fingernails are painted purple.]
LITERALLY SHATTERED

[Chris starts to take out accessories that were in the medallion.] We have uncovered the shrinky-dink. [annoyed sigh] It's okay, the pieces will be reserved in the updated one. It's another-nother piece of metal. [video cuts]
