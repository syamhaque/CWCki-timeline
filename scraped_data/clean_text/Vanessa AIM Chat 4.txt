Vanessa AIM Chat 4 is the fourth chat between Chris and Joshua Martinez, an old acquaintance pretending to be Vanessa Hudgens. It took place on 19 April 2009.
Summary
This chat mainly consists of Chris and Vanessa scheduling their meeting. Vanessa tries to win Chris over again and fails. He claims to be an empathetic person, and is loyal to Ivy apparently because she fixed his biological clock. The chat ends as Chris says that he's "felt True Love".
