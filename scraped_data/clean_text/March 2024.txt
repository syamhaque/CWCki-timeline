Notable events
1 March - Chris DMs an AI artist of My Little Pony over Twitter and gives a demand for that user to remove art depicting MLP G5 characters from their profile.[1]
2 March - Chris streams Christ Chan Live Stream: YouTube Video Watching.
5 March - Chris gets himself featured in the daily community soapbox of Equestria Daily, although the content is later scrubbed.
8 March - BABSCon emails Chris to inform him that he and his friend have been banned from the 2024 convention and all subsequent events. An upset Chris replies back but fails to persuade staff to undo the ban.[2]
9 March
Chris sulks on Twitter over his negative reputation[3] and tweets an angry remark about BABSCon.[4]
Chris livestreams Christ Chan Live Stream: Over the Shoulder Colouring and Drawing 2.
10 March - Chris records Telling It To All of You, and the Hasbro Executives and Staff.
16 March - Chris uploads AI Chris Chan Vs Christ Chan Bloom.
17 March - Chris marks Sonichu Day but tells his audience that he considers it the day he discovered Sonichu, and that the birthday is 20 April (a retconned belief he has held since the Idea Guys).[5]
18 March
Chris uploads Telling It To All of You, and the Hasbro Executives and Staff and rants yet again on MLP Generation 5.
Chris is spotted at River Ridge Mall being interviewed with TheGamerFromMars.[6]
Chris signs the Certificate of Authenticity for a round of official Sonichu Amiibo.[7]
22 March - Chris's last day actively liking posts on Twitter before an extended hiatus.[8]
23 March - Chris is spotted at TJ Maxx with Flutter.[9]
24 March - Chris uploads AI Chris Chan Vs Christ Chan - Yo Ya.
25 March
Chris is spotted at a parking lot near Target.[10]
Chris accidentally hits the livestream button - twice - when singing lyrics for another video.[11]
27 March - The first anniversary of Chris's release from Central Virginia Regional Jail.
31 March
A photo of the BABSCon E-mails surfaces on Twitter.
OfficialCWCmart starts selling the Sonichu Amiibo.[12]
