Highlights
1 September - ReBrick is merged into Lego Ideas.
2 September - Chris makes his first reference to dimension C-197, where Sonichu and other fictional characters exist. He also rambles on about his many variants.
11 September - Chris checks in with the Therapeutic Docket.[1]
12 September - Chris posts a Twitter rant about Barb.[2]
18 September - Chris gives advice to deal with anxiety by detaching himself from reality.[3]
22 September - Chris attends the local Gem, Mineral & Jewelry Show.
24 September
Second Round Sub, a debt collection agency, sues Chris for unpaid debt, set for 14 November.[4]
Chris attempts to force his headcanon on Bowsette, the Internet's latest sensation.[5]
25 September - Another check-in with the Therapeutic Docket.[1]
26 September - Chris enters the Newgrounds Bowsette/Super Crown art contest in hopes to win a Nintendo Switch.[6]
30 September - Chris earns his fastest platinum trophy on the PlayStation Network with the PS4 version of Little Adventure on the Prairie with a time of 36:34.
