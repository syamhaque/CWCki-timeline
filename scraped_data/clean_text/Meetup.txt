Dungeons & Dragons Harrisonburg
Profile picture for Dungeons & Dragons Harrisonburg
Dungeons & Dragons Harrisonburg is a tabletop roleplaying Meetup group based in Virginia. The group organizes games that can be played either in-person at local stores in Harrisonburg and Staunton or online from home. Chris joined the group in February 2020, likely in search of social interaction during the spreading of COVID-19.
