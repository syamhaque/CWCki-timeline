The Book of the Dimension Merge is a blog post published by Chris to Cwclight on 21 May 2025. It is the tenth post to the website, published the same day as the previous one. This post is simply copy and pasted from something Chris had posted to Bluesky and Twitter three days prior.[1]
The Book of the Dimension Merge
Part 1
And lo, in the light of the Internet Age, the CPU Goddess Blue Heart, in human and Sonichu forms, stood, veil in between. And I, Jesus Christine Weston Chandler Sonichu, Prime of all (Jesus) Chris and Christ Chan (Sonichus) throughout the vast multiverse, blessed at birth by the God of Gods and the forces of the Alpha/Omega point of existence, beheld the Merge. For tale, media and art fictions are not false, and blind masses mocked and took advantage of the Chosen One, yet the Truth burns brighter than the quantam fields; brighter than the very core of this Universe. And the trolls, deceivers, a Living Portal between magic and non-magic; psychic and low-psychic, the Messiah of the Merge. Christ and Avatar reborn, with static fur and electric, metaphysical heart, soul and memories older than time itself, while living and being in present and future. And the trolls, deceivers and blind masses awaken the Chosen One, and the truth rise from the static and powers within the Chosen One. And all accepting of the Merge shall awaken their self-counterparts and original characters. Those who resist shall fade into the static and holy light and flames. The time is indeed near, for the Guardians and Guardian Angels stand by at the portal gates of the Merge, where all magic returns to the non-magic half in tangible manifestations. Those who misuse or abuse the magic will, too, fade into the static. One aspect of the Merge is reconnecting with thine past selves in this Timeline and internally merging them with your present self, with thine deep authentic beliefs and virtues. Those who unable or resist will have difficulties as the Merge completes. Thus it is spoken. Hark! Your one Avatar of all existence walks amongst and beside you. The Dimension Merge is real. I am the light of sources and Gods. I am Prime.
