Chris in July 2017
July is the seventh month of 2017.
Highlights
1 July - An alternate cover and three colored-in pages of Sonichu 12 are posted.
4 July - Chris wishes his followers a happy Independence Day.
6 July - Chris posts about his "hot hamburger plate" dinner.
7 July - Chris films a Captain's Log, covering his progress on Sonichu 12 and his hope of gaining a sponsorship from Crayola.
9 July - Chris passes his first kidney stone.
14 July - Chris publishes CLog 07142017 - Rainbow Colouring.
22 July - Chris begs for $50 on Twitter to help him and Barb get through the rest of the week, and links to his PayPal. Other users try to persuade him to sell some of his junk.
23 July - Chris befriends a ween posing as DoopieDoOver on Facebook. The ween's account is disabled later that same day.
