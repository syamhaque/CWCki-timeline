Delusion disclosure: This article or section takes place during Chris's phase of role-playing as Sonichu. He may refer to his Christine Chandler personality and physical body in the third person by using terms such as "mama".
On 14 October 2020, Chris had a DM with Watchman leader Naught about his and some Praetor members' interpretation of the Disc Dream that he had.
The next day, the Praetor account, owned by Caden, joined The Place Discord server. Naught and Nova immediately start asking him questions while Chris steps away, mostly asking about their business and the finer details to their relationship with Chris. Praetor answers some of these, including tidbits on pricing and their creation process, though they refuse to disclose the split in income between themselves and Chris, claiming that Chris signed a confidentiality agreement. Praetor then asks questions of the Watchmen. Anaxis asks for social medias and they provide him with a Twitter and Instagram handle, as well as a business email. Chris returns and tells the others about him hand-writing the script for his upcoming sermon video, and complains about certain content not being easily found on the CWCki.
