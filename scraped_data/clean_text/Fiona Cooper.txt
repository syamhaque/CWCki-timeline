Fiona Cooper[6] (born circa 2002),[7][8][note 8] also known as Snoo, Fifi Vixenhart and "The Suitress", is self-described as Chris's "first real sweetheart."[9] Unlike most of Chris's other gal-pals, who at best were just friends or at worst left him for his unsavory traits, Fiona, seemingly out of some pity towards him,[10] had a genuine, admitted crush on him, so much so to the point of actually wanting to have sex with him,[11] even after the dark secret she'd later come to discover about him.[3]
Fiona was an active member of the CWCsphere, being a well-known member of communities such as the Official CWCki Server.[10] She tried to reach out to Chris via phone on October 2020, telling him about a drawing of Night Star she made. Still, she was warned against the interaction by a mod on the server.[12] However, despite repeated warnings, Fiona persisted and later achieved contact with Chris some time in mid-2021, around the time he made his announcement of going to the Brony convention Everfree Northwest. Fiona, along with Bella, was invited by Chris to attend with him to the convention as a means of protection from Jacob Sockness, who The WCT warned to him would attend in disguise. Fiona had decided that she'd have sex with Chris at the hotel that the trio would be staying at for the convention, and she made arrangements with him. Chris agreed to this request, believing that having sexual intercourse with her would help her "mature" when she meets a long-term boyfriend in college.
On 19 July 2021, Chris invited both Fiona and Bella into The Knights of CWC server. Bella introduced herself to Fiona and the two befriended each other. After Bella had a conversation with Chris about his affair with his "lady friend", she shared a clip of it with Fiona, who then shared an edited version of it to a group of people in another Discord server she was in, leading to the call getting leaked and causing a massive fallout amongst the CWCsphere. When Bella told Fiona about Chris's incestuous affair, and shared the Incest Call and Incest Texts, Fiona then shared a censored version of the Call with the Chris Chan Conservation Collective server, omitting Bella's side of the conversation. When server members doubted the authenticity of the original leak, she posted Bella's voice without her permission.[13] After Chris Chan Conservation Collective member EllDudeRocks leaked the Incest Call to the general public, Fiona leaked the Incest Texts to Reddit, despite Bella having told her not to post them yet.[14]
Though she initially tried to protect Fiona from the Knights following the leaks by claiming that Fiona was a minor, Bella later attempted to frame her as being the voice in the call in her frantic attempts to avoid being doxed. This move backfired on Bella, as it had led to gossipers on Kiwi Farms led by Spooky Bones perceiving Fiona as a victim of her's, giving them more justification to vilify Bella and leading to Fiona getting obfuscated on several platforms. After the ensuing drama, expressing feeling traumatized both by his Chris's crime and his arrest, Fiona distanced herself from Chris before posting more leaks on a throwaway account two weeks later. Despite this, Chris has mentioned a desire to get back in contact with her in jail letters and several videos.

Aliases
Fiona had went under a number of aliases during her time in the CWCSphere.
During her online presence, Fiona was most known under the alias "Snoo", assuming the avatar of Reddit's alien mascot. She used this alias when she was first on the CWCki Discord server.[15] She also had an account on Reddit under the alias "IanBwandonAndOWOson", which she used to post pictures and videos of herself cosplaying as Chris. After being invited to the Knights of CWC server, she went under "ScootalooSister", likely because Chris invited her to Everfree Northwest.
When she began interacting with Chris, Fiona went under the pseudonym "Fifi Vixenhart." After meeting with Fiona, Chris referred to Fiona by this pseudonym when talking with his other associates. [16] Since his arrest, Chris has referred to Fiona as "Fifi" as a sort of pet name.

The codename "The Suitress" (previously "The Everfree Suitress") was created by the CWCki editor and jerkop PsychoNerd054 on 15 August 2021.[17] This was done in response to the stigma surrounding Fiona getting doxed after Bella framed her, which involved Spooky Bones; the leading figure in the investigations of Bella; threatening to dox or otherwise target people who tried to dox Fiona.[18] In some cases, this happened, such as with The WCT, who was manipulated by Bella into framing her, and GiBi, who inadvertently leaked Fiona's phone number in a video showcasing texts Bella gave him. Several CWCki staff, which included Hurtful Truth Level and Marvin, were also subjects of scrutiny due to both of them being in The WCT's Knights of CWC server, which Bella and Fiona also happened to be in. Both Hurtful and Marvin were also criticized for supposedly doing nothing to stop Bella from framing Fiona, despite that fact that neither of them were in the Regarding Chris chats, where the framing took place.
Psycho chose to attempt to censor Fiona on CWCki pages. He also drafted a policy for Fiona censorship, called the Suitress Policy.[19] It took inspiration from the CWCki's Wallflower Policy, however differed from it in that Fiona did not request the censorship.[20] Fiona herself rejected the censorship attempt. On r/ChrisChanSonichu, she posted a selfie to prove her Reddit account; leaked Chris Chan's Good Morning, an audio file in which Chris sang her name; then created an AMA thread titled, "I'm Fiona, A.K.A "The Everfree Suitress" and I genuinely had a crush on Chris Chan. Ask me anything and I'll respond on my own time."[21][22]
The policy was lessened a bit on 27 March 2022 after Hurtful had discussed it with Psycho, concerned about the issues that were arising from the amount of censorship that was being done on Fiona. Both of them agreed to lessen the policy over time before getting rid of it entirely.[23] This change then allowed users to utilize Fiona's other screen names and show her face in videos. In September 2023, Anaxis wrote a case against the Suitress policy, and he and other CWCki editors agreed to overturn it and restore information previously censored after discussing it.[24] However, the codename was kept as a unifying nickname for Fiona.
Chris referenced the Suitress alias at one point in his jail letters, when he wrote that "she is not a suitress".[25]
Psycho began writing the essay "The Blind Observers and a Snoo" on 4 May 2025, as a way to explain both his thoughts on the drama surrounding Fiona and his reasoning behind the policy.[26]

Early Relations to Chris
Prior to interacting with Chris, Fiona discovered him by happenstance through several documentaries and video essays that covered him, as many fans her age had. From there, Fiona had seemingly come to relate to Chris in some ways, seeing parts of herself in him, and she later became a well-known and active member of the CWCsphere. This fascination that Fiona had in Chris later developed into a crush towards him, and, according to one of her IRL friends,[33] she had expressed an innate desire to meet him in person. Despite the insistence other people had that she not interact with Chris, she was determined to do so, and was finally able to achieve contact with him by mid-2021.
