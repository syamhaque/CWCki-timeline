Cwclight Reviews Opening Intros with Subtitles is a video uploaded to Chris's YouTube channel on 21 November 2025. It is a compilation video of all the intros of Chris's Transformers toy review videos he made so far. He uploaded a version without subtitles a minute later. The ending skit of Light Powered Cwclight Reviews - Gwenmi Mikoto is also included in the video for some reason.
