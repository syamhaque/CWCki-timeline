Notable events
6 December - Chris posts indicating that he has gone to retail stores to pretend that his custom Transformers toys are on the shelves.[1]
7 December - Light Powered Cwclight Reviews - Son-Chu‎ is published.
14 December - Light Powered Cwclight Reviews - Silver is published.
17 December - Chris and Flutter are spotted back in Finland, confirming their third known trip to the country.[2]
19 December - Light Powered Cwclight Reviews - Excelina is published.
25 December - Christmas, and Chris's second observance of the holiday since his release.
