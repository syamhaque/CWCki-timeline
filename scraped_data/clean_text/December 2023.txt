Notable events
2 December - Chris roasts Elon Musk on Twitter.[1]
3 December
Chris's Chevrolet Aveo is sighted at Boonsboro Shopping Center in Lynchburg, VA.[2]
Chris finishes drawing a holiday commission.[3]
5 December - Chris is spotted and photographed at the Greene County Sheriff's Office, apparently trying to reclaim some of his items.[4]
11 December - Chris makes a post on Twitter in which he advocates for sexual predators to be welcomed back into communities.[5]
13 December
Toys for Tots is uploaded to Chris's YouTube channel.
Chris asks that if people want to send him Christmas gifts, he would prefer them to be legitimate and not gag gifts.[6]
14 December
Chris livestreams Fan Mail and Cwcville Autobots.
Chris makes numerous posts on Twitter, including one criticizing Geno Samuel's knockoff 3D-printed Sonichu medallions.[7]
16 December - Chris is seen and photographed at a UPS store.[8]
19 December - Chris uploads Transformers - Past Ideas Reemerging Part 1 to YouTube.
20 December
Chris and Flutter are filmed at Richmond International Airport.[9]
Chris is spotted and photographed with Flutter at a Sheetz. [10]
21 December - Chris and Flutter are spotted and photographed while eating dinner at Isabella's Italian Trattoria Restaurant in Lynchburg, VA.[11]
22 December - Chris livestreams Over the shoulder colouring stream, coloring two pages of Sonichu 0 HD.
25 December - C-Mas: His first Christmas spent out of jail since 2020, and his third in a row since his removal from 14BC.
30 December - Transformers - Past Ideas Reemerging Part 2 is uploaded to Chris's YouTube channel.
