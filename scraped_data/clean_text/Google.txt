Google LLC is an American multinational technology company focusing on artificial intelligence, online advertising, search engine technology, cloud computing, computer software, quantum computing, e-commerce, and consumer electronics. It has been referred to as "the most powerful company in the world" and as one of the world's most valuable brands due to its market dominance, data collection, and technological advantages in the field of artificial intelligence. Google's parent company Alphabet Inc. is one of the five Big Tech companies, alongside Amazon, Apple Inc., Meta, and Microsoft.
Google offers a wide number of products, services, and apps. Of relevance to Christory are YouTube - where Chris uploads videos and interacts with trolls, Google Drive - which he has used to privately send videos, and Google Maps - where he has left reviews for several locations.

Google Drive
Chris has occasionally uploaded videos to his Google Drive for privately sharing.
On 9 March 2018, Chris recorded the Tour of Chris's House III video for the Idea Guys. A portion of the video was leaked by the Teen Troon Squad on 11 December 2018, which they were able to acquire since Chris did not delete the file.[1] Two years later, on 11 August 2020, the Watchmen member Klop posted both the full video and the original Google Drive link to it,[2] showing Chris had still not deleted the file. Eventually, the file was deleted from the Drive.
On 6 August 2020, Chris recorded a video on his Last Will and Testament and uploaded to his Drive account. The video link was later leaked by Naught to the CWCki.
Google Hangout
