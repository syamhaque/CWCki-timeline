Chris and Flutter in April, spotted in Finland.
April was the fourth month of 2024. It was a fairly quiet month for Chris outside of some YouTube uploads, but it is notable for marking the first time Chris ever traveled outside of the United States; it wouldn't be revealed until months later, but Chris had flown to Finland with Flutter this month.

Notable events
4 April - Chris and Flutter are spotted in Finland.[1]
5 April - Chris uploads AI Chris Chan Vs Christ Chan - Silakka Apajalla.
13 April - Chris uploads AI Chris Chan Vs Christ Chan - Pray High Valyrian.
14 April - After over three weeks, Chris returns to Twitter to complain over leaked chats.[2]
22 April - Chris uploads Harvesting Process, Gravel at the Sonichu Temple.
26 April - Chris uploads Christ Chan - Sakkijarven Polkka mp4, his first singing video without the AI, as apparently the song was too difficult for it.
28 April - Chris was seen online on Discord through 7:49 PM - 8:16 PM EST.
29 April - Chris was seen online on Discord through 6:40 PM - 6:52 PM EST.
30 April
Chris and Flutter enjoy a visit to Linnanmäki together, the biggest amusement park in Finland.[3] It was the park's first day of reopening for the season.
Chris was seen online on Discord through 11:13 PM - 11:36 PM EST.
Chris's incest case is updated with miscellaneous court information.[4]
