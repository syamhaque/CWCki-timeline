The Watchmen again ask Praetor about the taser. Earlier in the day, Chris had mentioned wanting to use a taser to spark his collection of crystals to activate the Dimensional Merge, which Praetor said they would temporarily supply him with.[2]
The discussion begins poorly, with Watchmen members Val and MKR using name-calling and other hostile remarks towards Praetor member Caden. Caden responds with mocking remarks.
Chris tries to defuse the situation, wanting the hostility to stop. Watchmen member Kyle jumps in and accuses Caden of fraud, to which Chris disagrees and responds that there is no fraud.
Hostilities from Val spill over to verbally attacking Chris as well, calling him an idiot.
The discussion continues via the Taser Discussion voice chat.
Chris posts his thoughts on how he felt overwhelmed from the aggression, recalling instances from his childhood in which he had felt aggrieved, and saying he interpreted the remarks as war-like instead of comical banter, and that he would try not to take future remarks personally.
Following the discussion, MKR took to Twitter to spread a rumor that Praetor wanted to get Chris to use a stun gun on himself. To fuel the rumor, she cropped an out-of-context portion of the discussion.[3]
