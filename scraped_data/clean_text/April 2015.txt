Title: April 2015
URL: https://sonichu.com/cwcki/April_2015
Categories: Category, Chronology
================================================================================

On 2 April 2015, Chris gets spotted outside the Charlottesville Circuit Court.
On 6 April 2015, Chris creates some Sonichu Amiibo.
April was the 4th month of 2015. Chris's third court date following 26 December 2014 took place, with his case to be continued on 7 May. He also revealed a new product on eBay, a Sonichu Amiibo. He continued to clumsily support gay rights and bitterly complain about Sonic the Hedgehog's blue arms.
Events of April 2015
On 13 April 2015, Chris sports a new muscle bra.
1 April
April Fools' Day. To celebrate, the CWCki eliminates the front page and replaces it with a tongue-in-cheek disclaimer about how we need to respect Chris.
On Facebook, Chris calls TV Land's Younger "Modernly Tech Savvy and smart".
2 April - Chris's second court hearing since the 26 December 2014 incident.
4 April - Chris rages over the two-part season 5 premiere of My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic and a character in the Equestria Girls: Rainbow Rocks movie.
5 April - Easter.
6 April - Chris announces ten custom Sonichu Amiibo for sale on eBay. His fans buy them all in less than two hours.
7 April
Chris provides more information on the custom Amiibo.
Chris asks for people to vote in a Nintendo contest to make Sonichu a downloadable character in Super Smash Bros. for Nintendo 3DS/Wii U.
8 April - A fan attempts to visit Chris to give him two animation frames from the Adventures of Sonic the Hedgehog cartoon, but gets told off by Barb. It is not known if Chris knows that his mother prevented him from getting these frames.
12 April - On Facebook, Chris supports Hillary Clinton for president, and opposes the hunting of giraffes.
13 April
Chris says that Disney Infinity is mediocre.
Chris "contributes" to the Free the Nipple movement...by taking a ridiculous selfie of himself in a turban and a bra.
15 April - Chris makes a suggestion on Facebook about a piece of Equestria Girls fan art.
16 April
Chris claims that if Sonichu gets into Super Smash Bros. for 3DS/WU as DLC, he will make new Sonichu comics.
In an unrelated Facebook post, Chris expresses his conviction that transgender people should be allowed to use the public restroom of their choice.
17 April
Chris refutes a Daily Mail UK article saying that Richmond, Virginia is the happiest place in the United States. He lists many people he perceives as enemies, counters that it is a "paranoid" and "illsocial" place, and abuses hashtags.
An Australian fan posts a YouTube review of his received Sonichu Amiibo.
20 April
Chris is pissed that Knuckles' Chaotix and Sonic CD haven't been ported to modern consoles or Virtual Console. (Note that Sonic CD has been ported to, among other things, the iPhone.)
Chris complains about a Minecraft rip-off introduced for the Wii U, foreshadowing the discovery of his Minecraft account two days later.
Chris once again complains about Sonic's blue arms on Facebook.
In other Facebook news, Chris officially declares that he uses feminine pronouns.
21 April
Chris chooses a new Facebook profile pic.
Chris comments that a girl featured in an AnimatedJames webtoon is a "cute lesbian."
On Facebook, Chris notes that he signed a petition to get justice for a middle-school-age autistic boy who had been charged with felony assault.
Chris calls for gay marriage on Facebook.
22 April
On Facebook, Chris shares and comments on a news report about a five-year-old transgender boy.
It is discovered that Chris has begun playing Minecraft, under the name "ChristineWeston."
23 April - Chris uploads two videos of his avatar, named Christine, playing Mario Kart 8. He provides a link to one of them on the Miiverse.
25 April
In the third of a weekly series, Chris discusses how he'd like Black Sonichu to be integrated into Super Smash Bros.
On Facebook, Chris says that he loves his family and misses his father.
Chris makes an Abbott and Costello reference on Facebook, probably because an episode of My Little Pony referenced it the same day. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wLDGvPRrzCA
28 April - Chris wonders what would've happened if Sonic Boom had been named Sonic Synergy, and mentions that he has mixed feelings towards the Sonic CD song "Sonic Boom", despite it appearing on the Three-Quarter Woman CD.
29 April - Chris returns to the PSN for the first time since December 2014 to play Little Pig Blanket 3.
