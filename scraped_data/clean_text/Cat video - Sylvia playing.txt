Cat video - Sylvia playing is a video Chris uploaded to YouTube on 17 November 2018. The video opens on Chris's cat Sylvia playing with a cat toy that is held by Chris who is off camera. The backdrop for the video is the sign from the Dreaming Studio propped up against a table in Chris's room. Throughout the short video, Chris makes candid remarks about Sylvia while she jumps at the toy Chris is holding.
