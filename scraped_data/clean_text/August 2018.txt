Chris in August 2018
August was the eighth month of 2018. Chris spent most of this month e-begging, uploading no less than three videos where Barb takes the stage and tells the viewers to buy their stuff.

Highlights
3 August - Chris uploads a four-page comic, The Awakening of a CPU, AKA The Idea Guy Corruptions, and records Shoe Colouring.
5 August - Chris disputes Null's account of Anna and Rocky in a Kiwi Farms thread on the ousting of the Idea Guys, also objecting to his vulgarity towards the two. Chris asks that Null apologize, however, he deletes the tweets 20 minutes later[1] after Null and The Captain threaten to withdraw their support.[2]
10 August - The first of the known Teen Troon Squad chats, in which Chris describes beating up and humping a body pillow in effigy of his mother.
13 August
Chris is photographed in the waiting room of the Region 10 therapist wearing a gaudy and childish outfit.[3]
Chris uploads Hasbro! MLP G4 Must Continue; NO MLP G5, ranting about seeing the future and commanding Hasbro not to proceed with rebooting MLP.
14 August - Chris comments on the prior day's photo of him at Region 10.[4]
15 August - Chris attempts primal scream therapy.
18 August - Chris records himself making funny noises and uploads it on Twitter.
20 August - Barb advertises for autographed posters in Barbara Chandler will be making out autographed photos and posters.
28 August - Chris's Trespass after Forbidden hearing resumes, ending with a continuance for 11 September.
29 August - Chris uploads Mother needs a car payment‎‎, his third begging video in a row to feature Barb.
