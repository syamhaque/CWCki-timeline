Hermaphroditism is the biological condition where an organism possesses reproductive organs and/or gametes of both sexes.[3] The term was also once used to refer to people who were intersex.
Chris, however, specifically applied the term "hermaphrodite" to refer to any person that he perceived as being "female" or of an ambiguous gender, but with a penis and secondary female sex characteristics (such as breasts). This included transgender women that hadn't received bottom surgery, as well as intersex people that he perceived as being female. In essence, Chris used the term the same way some people would refer to such people as "shemales", "dickgirls", or "futa". Like with a lot of other things, Chris's understanding of trans and intersex people came from the media he's consumed. His initial understanding of such concepts mirrored those of many other people during the late 2000s and early 2010s. Chris had seemingly conflated transgenderism with intersexuality, believing that both terms referred to the same thing.
Chris more often than not used the term "hermaphrodite" as a pejorative towards trans women, under the fear that they too can make him gay, due to their supposed ability to subvert his straightness. During the Classic Era, Chris was also noted for being peeved by his female characters being portrayed as "hermaphrodites", being portrayed with penises or as trans women. For these reasons, the way Chris used the term can be thought of as the transgender equivalent of "homos". Chris has also used the term "homo", as well as "transvestite" and "lady-man", as demeaning terms towards trans women that he he didn't deem authentic, and refused to respect the identities of.[4][5] These past attitudes of Chris's however, are far overshadowed amongst the CWCsphere by his recent, more vocal support for trans people on social media.
Chris similarly used the term "intersex" to refer to the same thing, but in a less derogatory manner, using the term on himself when he began transitioning,[6] as well as for the characters Rosechu and Sylvana, both of whom had penises at one point or another.

Before his transition, Chris tried to maintain his "straightness" by acting like what he thought a stereotypical man acted like, showing insecurity of his more effeminate traits. This was likely a result of the aforementioned fact that he believed that one straying away too much from their assigned gender made them gay, which meant that Chris believed that he's proving to the trolls that he's gay by showcasing his femineity, and so took every possible opportunity while recording himself to subvert it by feigning masculinity.
When Chris was forced to admit that he was gay by a group of trolls, he held up an action figure of Optimus Prime, who he deemed as a symbol of masculinity, throughout the entire video.[32] Chris had also greatly exaggerated his physical prowess, usually to appear more powerful than his trolls or to impress his gal-pals. Despite all of of Chris's efforts, though, they were completely overshadowed by his penchant for crossdressing, something he was very defensive about. The earliest known instance of this recorded is with the "muscle bra". Chris has stated that all men should wear "muscle bras" to cover their chests by law, being uncomfortable with seeing the chest of a man. On 28 April 2011, Surfshack Tito also leaked a set of photos, which highlighted Chris's eventual gender transition. Even while the facade was on its last legs, Chris still tried to assure people that he was a heterosexual man who was just "in touch with his feminine side".
Chris, after transitioning, has ironically become one of the things he once winced at the thought of. However, from doing this, his gender dysphoria became much more apparant, and he had an inverse perspective on his presentation, now wanting to eliminate all masculine traits of himself, including his penis. Chris only appears to view those who have a vagina as "fully female", regardless of their chromosomes. This also seemed to be a driving force of his that led to him taking such drastic measures on himself, and expressing self-loathing on himself for being born male. Shifting Stance on Trans People
Chris's Facebook profile picture in March 2017
Nowadays, despite his far more obscure history with regards to trans people, Chris is widely known as a trans advocate, making many social media posts about the rights of trans people, and implementing trans characters in his comics. He's shown such sentiments since at least 2015, a year after he openly transitioned. On social media, Chris was noted for often times agreeing with prominent transgender figures.
Before officially transitioning, when Chris had declared himself a tomgirl, his stance towards his characters being transgender also shifted more positively. The most prominent example of this being Robee (formerly Robbie, now Roberta), who was initially written as being the youngest son of Sonichu and Rosechu. Chris decided to write an about Robee's transition in Sonichu 12, making it the main focus of the story "To Be or Naught a Tomgirl", which showed his decision to do so during his childhood, his evolution, and eventual transition. He similarly made Cera Rosechu and Christine Rosechu into tomboys. He also showed his support to the trans community by starting up a new band, Robee Sonee and the Tomgirls, consisting of the three of them.
On 16 April 2015, Chris had found an article on a recent episode of Ellen DeGeneres' talk show that dealt with transgender rights. There, Chris states that trans people should use whatever bathrooms best aligns with their gender identities.[37]
Chris also follows LGBT News, which sometimes covers transgender-related topics. In May 2017, he commented on a post from them which linked to a theoretical article about a possible surgery for trans-women to become pregnant.[38]
