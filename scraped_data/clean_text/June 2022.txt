"Prideful Interview With God"
June was the sixth month of 2022. Chris spent the month in jail, sending a few letters and drawing several Praetor Jail Art images.
Notable events
1 June - Chris writes and sends a second letter to Helena Fiorenza.
4 June - Jail Letter - 4 June 2022 is sent.
5 June - Chris draws An Honest Self-Portrait from June, 2021.
6 June - Jail Letter - 9 May 2022 and Jail Letter - 1 June 2022 are posted.
9 June - Chris's incest case receives another continuance.[1]
10 June - Jail Letter - 4 June 2022 from Eels and the Egg-man is released.
13 June - Jail Letter - 13 June 2022 is sent to Eels and the Egg-man.
15 June - Lego announces they are building their first American factory, located 90 minutes away from 14 Branchland Court in Chesterfield County.[2]
19 June - Chris draws "Val Sonichu's Coffee Break" and "Prideful Interview With God".
25 June - Jail Letter - 25 June 2022 is sent to Eels and the Egg-man.
25 June - Chris draws "An Atlantic Dip".
26 June - Chris draws "COME_ADORE_BASIC_REVALATIONS_LOAD, 8, 1".
27 June - The one year anniversary of the beginning of Chris's affair with his own mother.[3][4]
27 June - Chris writes a short letter to Eels, saying that he expects to be released soon and that Barb was never interviewed by police.
28 June - Chris draws "A (Dragon fruit) Tea With Sylvania Rosechu".
29 June
Chris draws "Sun of Righteousness" and "Tea Talk With Cryzel Rosechu".
Chris draws Wedded Schultzes using a receipt from the jail commissary as scrap paper, revealing some details of his commissary balance.
