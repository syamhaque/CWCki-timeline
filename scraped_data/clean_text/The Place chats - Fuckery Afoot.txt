On 29 March 2021, after all of the drama surrounding "Imposter Lainchu", Chris has a private conversation with Naught in a DM about Molly the Medium, who he confirms was the one who inspired him to make both the Lovers Attributes and Affirmations lists, who were both mistakenly believed to be Anaxis' doing from the other Watchmen. Chris clarified that he very vaguely told Anaxis about it.[note 1] Despite this, Naught seemingly put more focus on Chris's brief mention of Anaxis, and took this as an indication of there being "fuckery afoot".
MKRNightVee replies to this, hoping that Anaxis "makes friends with a noose (in Minecraft)", and the other Watchmen presume Anaxis a "fail troll" that will leak Chris's conversations when he's bored.
