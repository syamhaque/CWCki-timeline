Title: February 2011
URL: https://sonichu.com/cwcki/February_2011
Categories: Category, Chronology
================================================================================

Chris advertises his Autism Tutorial.
February was the second month of 2011. Chris turned 29, spent the majority of the month playing LittleBigPlanet 2, and completed his half-hour "Autism Tutorial". Also, more background information was revealed about past sweethearts Kacey and Ivy.
Events
8 February - Chris leaves a message on his LittleBigPlanet 2 account begging for a change on the CWCki main page.[1]
12 February - A YouTube user uploads a video showing CWC's Collector's Case Tutorial.
13 February
The Ivy Q&A is released.
A video of Chris's LBP2 Autism Tutorial is released.
24 February - Chris's 29th birthday.
25 February
Jackie sends Chris a birthday greeting through email and asks about his well-being.
Sonichu goes for an entire year without a single new page uploaded (excluding the "corrected" ending).
26 February - The final phone call between Chris and Kacey is released.
