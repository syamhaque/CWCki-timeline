Val's OC, Val Valentino
Val Valentino was an OC created by AquaDiamond8, being her interpretation of Magi-Chan. Several people took on the role of the character, including Aqua herself and a Watchman who would later refer to themselves as "Val".
Mentions
Val's avatar appears to have been drawn by Helena Fiorenza, as confirmed in a TSSSF card. In June 2022, Chris mentioned Val in Jail Letter - 25 June 2022, attempting to have him and Helena talk to each other.
