CWCville Shopping was a website created by Chris on 23 November 2019 to sell his custom Twilight Sparkle's Secret Shipfic Folder cards and, on 17 December 2019, his official Sonichu comics and two fan comics. The site, much like his others, was extremely bare-bones and poorly designed. The home page featured a backdrop of the first eight Sonichu comics yet the only products available at launch were the card packs. They were all quite expensive; Chris cited the cost of printing for this.[3] Chris used a website called Make Playing Cards.com or MPC for his print on demand, similar to his use of Lulu when selling his comics on Etsy.[4][5] Chris's website was created with Weebly.
As of 30 August 2020, the website is offline. In a Twitter post from 28 September 2020, Chris stated that the site was closed since he wasn't able to pay the monthly fee to keep it up on time.[6]

Doxing of customers
One of many images containing sensitive customer information Chris had sent to Naught.
Chris has doxed many of his own customers to various Watchmen members.
On 7 September 2020, Chris DM'd Naught and ordered him to fulfill a backlog of twenty-three orders ranging from June-August 2020. Chris then screencapped the information of those customers - including their names, phone numbers, addresses, and emails - and forwarded the information to Naught, who then posted the material in The Place.[7][8]
In late September 2020, a disgruntled customer who had yet to receive his order, Justin, angrily confronted Chris over text messages. Chris shot back at Justin, then leaked his texts to The Place, which included Justin's personal information.[9]
