Highlights
1 April - Chris texts MKR to discuss that he had crashed his car into a truck and damaged it the previous month.[1]
2 - 4 April - BABSCon, an unofficial My Little Pony convention held in San Francisco, was to be held but was delayed another year.
3 April - A fan snaps a photo of Chris's car, showing the damage it has sustained from his fender bender the previous month.[2]
4 April - Another fan inquires about the damage to Chris's car; he explains it was from backing into a big rig trailer the previous week.[3]
5 April - Chris texts MKR to discuss an "almost-sex dream" and a sex fantasy with the adult version of Christopher Thorndyke, a character from Sonic media. He also mentions feeling sex-starved.[4]
12 April
Chris does tarot readings for Naught and The WCT.[5]
Null gives an update to Kiwi Farms users on his plans for a Kiwi Farms silver coin featuring Chris's likeness.[6]
15 April - A listing of supposed meditation crystals and enchanted rocks is released on Praetor's OfficialCWCmart.
24 April - Chris talks about numerology in The Place, showing a book he had bought on the subject and his self-analysis based on it.[7]
26 April - Chris posts on Instagram that he got his stimulus check, and purchased a 3-month subscription (which is $165) to The Crystal Council, a subscription box service focused on crystals and rocks.[8]
28 April - Chris gets his first dose of the Pfizer COVID-19 vaccination, while Barb gets her second dose of Pfizer.[9]
