"Brutal Legend Plus" is a video uploaded by Chris on 21 October 2009. It was shot and released 3 days after Chris made a video with a similar name, but only just then realized that the video he shot prior had no audio at all.
The video also features an additional message that invoked much rage and lulz from the online community.
Due to the heavy abuse Chris's camera endured during the last month, the sound is crappy as hell and the video is skipping quite a lot. The plush breasts are still in the left bottom of the screen; they have not moved since the last video.
