================================================================================

Collector's Case Tutorial is a level that Chris uploaded to the LittleBigPlanet Community on 9 February 2011. In it, Chris (playing the role of Sonichu) details how to build and use a collector's case.
Until 2015, when Chris picked up the series again, the CWC's Collector's Case Tutorial was the last work of Chris to significantly feature a Sonichu character. While Sonichu and Rosechu would be seen in the Autism Tutorial (released on 13 February 2011), they had no speaking roles and merely appeared in the background.
