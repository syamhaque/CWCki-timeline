Chris isn't much of a cook, nor were any of the current or former residents of his previous house, 14 Branchland Court. When Chris isn't eating reheated frozen dinners, he's usually chowing down at a variety of local restaurants. Keeping with the theme of commercial consumerism, all of Chris's favorite restaurants are chains.
Many of these restaurants do not offer many, or any, meals that provide good nutrition.

Applebee's
Applebee's is a popular chain of sit-down restaurants. Chris has occasionally eaten here, but it's generally too dear for his budget. His most notable visit was his date with Catherine and her cousin Al on 31 October 2014, where he horrified staff and patrons alike. During the father call, he mentions taking Kacey there and leaving a 15% tip on a bill of (somehow) ten dollars â€“ a whole $1.50. Arby's
Arby's is a large quick-service sandwich chain known for having the meats. In one IRC chat, Chris considered Arby's as one of the possible restaurants to which he will take his first date.
Arnold's
Arnold's is a chain of coffee shops in Finland. Chris and Flutter were seen eating in one in April 2025.[1]
Baja Bean
Baja Bean was a small chain serving Mexican food. A look at their website suggests they now have only one restaurant.[2] Interestingly, their location in Richmond was located only blocks from where Barb raised Cole Smithey near campus of Virginia Commonwealth University. They also had a location in Charlottesville that Chris is known to have visited[3], but this has since closed down.
Chris tried to set up a meeting with Regina at Baja Bean.[4]
Burger King
Main article: Burger King
Chris favors McDonald's, whereas Bob preferred its rival, Burger King. Thus, Chris usually only ate here when he was out with his father.
Chick-fil-A
Admittedly, the store might've been a literary influence.
Chick-fil-A is a fast-food restaurant chain originating in the American South that Chris frequents, famous for its chicken sandwiches and lemonade and for always being closed on Sundays (due to their founder's Christian "seventh day of rest" beliefs). Chick-fil-A is generally considered to be one of the healthier fast food options in the United States.[5] However, Chris has mentioned it much less often than its artery-clogging big brother, McDonald's. The reason for this is unknown; possibilities include its lack of marketing campaigns aimed at children or an absence of foolish sweepstakes or "reward programs". As such, Chris has never been banned from a Chick-fil-A in an epic showdown battling jerkops. Even when the company came under fire for its founder's anti-homosexuality views and its involvements in "family values" movements, both of which would likely endear it to Chris, it did not seem to curry any favor with him.
Chris met up with Emily in front of the Chick-fil-A at Charlottesville Fashion Square. Or at least tried to by calling out to the patrons (who were undoubtedly amused and perplexed).
The Fashion Square Chick-fil-A was the site of one of the missed meetings between Chris and Surfshack Tito's agents during the drama over destroying Chris's PS3.
Chris and Flutter ate at Chick-fil-A in River Ridge Mall in June 2024.[6]
The CWCville Shopping Center includes a lone Chick-fil-A on the second floor.
Collegeville Italian Bakery
Collegeville Italian Bakery Pizzeria Napoletana is an Italian restaurant in the Philadelphia suburb of Collegeville. Chris visited on 23 June 2018 after he was booted from the TooManyGames convention for inappropriate behavior. He plugged the restaurant on Twitter and suggested a new pizza called "The Sonichu Zap!" made with yellow peppers.[7]
Country Cookin
Simonla calls for Country Cookin.
Country Cookin was Chris's not-quite-fast-food restaurant of choice. It was a Virginia-based chain of rustic steak house/buffet and grill restaurants, akin to Golden Corral, Roadhouse Grill or Ponderosa/Bonanza Steakhouse in the US, and Harvester, Beefeater or Brewer's Fayre in the United Kingdom.
Chris had mentioned it on several occasions and even had taken his troll friend Joshua Martinez there.
The company closed all of its locations in October 2020 as a result of the COVID-19 pandemic.
In an e-mail conversation with Anna McLerran from 2007, Chris mentioned his regular trips to the restaurant with Joshua.
In Mumble 2, Chris mentioned Country Cookin as one of his favorite restaurants.
A Country Cookin branch is located in the CWCville Shopping Center.
In Sonichu #11, a newly-resurrected Simonla recommends going for some Country Cookin food.
In a 2011 email to LoveYouLongTime, Chris said he had tried an "Autistic Treatment" ("Silent, Deaf and No Eye-Contact") during a family dinner with his parents at Country Cookin and that doing so had "wrecked" the dinner. He had done so in a vain attempt to manipulate them into allowing him to proceed with his plan to go to MAGfest despite not having budgeted money for it.[8]
Crozet Pizza
A pizzeria located in Crozet, Virginia, a town 12 miles (19 km) west of Charlottesville. Anna McLerran had her 20th birthday party here in August 2005, which Chris attended and allegedly helped to plan.
El Agave
A Mexican restaurant chain with a location in Ruckersville where Chris and his parents have eaten.
In Jackie Chat 8, Chris mentions going to El Agave with his parents using his tugboat money.
In September 2013, Chris scraped a woman's car while backing up in the El Agave parking lot.[9][10]
The $500 gift card shows that Chris made four separate visits to El Agave between 19 September and 30 September 2015, spending a total of $39.88.[11]
Golden Corral
Golden Corral is an all-you-can-eat buffet chain, well-known in the American South and Midwest as the place to go for a maximum ratio of calories consumed to cash expended. Just don't think too much about where it all came from. During Chris's date with Emily, Bob mentions that he had a heart attack there while paying for the family's meal, but was caught by Barb before he fell to the floor.[12] Despite this, it was one of Chris's favorite places to eat, probably since he could stuff his face with fatty, low-quality food at a low price. However, the Charlottesville branch went out of business in 2010[13] and became a Chinese buffet.[14]
Caitlin, a waitress Chris had a crush on for a day or so, worked at Golden Corral.
In Mumble 2, he mentioned that it's one of his favorite places to eat.
Describing his ideal date spots in an IRC chat, Chris proposed Golden Corral as a second-date destination.
Chris's visits to the Corral with his family seem to be tightly scheduled. In a MySpace conversation from 4 September 2005, he noted that they'd be there from 3:30 to 4:45 the next day.
Perhaps because of that precise schedule, or perhaps because he was simply overwhelmed by stress, during his final Skype conversation with BlueSpike Chris unexpectedly abandoned the conversation to take off for the Corral.
Chris listed it as his favorite restaurant in his profile at The HooK Cafe.
In a May 2014 Facebook post, responding to news that a fire had destroyed the Chinese restaurant where the Golden Corral once stood, he angrily demanded that his favorite restaurant be rebuilt on the site.
IHOP
A restaurant chain best known for its pancakes and other breakfast foods. Chris's parents would go there for dinner every week during the classic Chris era to take advantage of the senior discount.[15] Chris was usually not brought along (ostensibly because it would disqualify them from the discount), but his parents would get takeout with their meal and bring it home to him afterwards. Chris was fond of their grilled chicken sandwiches.[16][17]
Chris discusses IHOP with Julie in BlueSpike PSN Chat 1.
Chris discusses IHOP with Kacey in Kacey Call 6. Kacey takes issue with the fact that Chris's parents go to a pancake house for sandwiches.
Chris mentions IHOP in Kacey Call 11.
Chris mentions IHOP in DayOut.
In Common Questions Chris mentions IHOP as one of the restaurants he goes to besides McDonald's or Burger King.
In Jackie Chat 8 Chris claims that he treated his parents to dinner at IHOP twice in one month using his tugboat money. Since Chris has previously said that his parents routinely go there without him and that it is cheaper for them to pay for the food using their senior discount, this claim is dubious.
In the Rebeckah Bentley E-mails Chris mentions going to IHOP on his 32nd birthday for the free ice cream dinner.
Isabella's Italian Trattoria Restaurant
An Italian restaurant in Lynchburg, VA. Chris and Flutter were spotted and photographed eating dinner there in December 2023 by Redditor JacksonStarship, who posted about it to r/ChrisChanSonichu. JacksonStarship claimed that Chris ate spaghetti like a toddler and that Flutter paid for the meal and kept Chris's behavior in check.[18]
Matt's Pub & Comedy Club
A pub in the Shockoe Slip district of Richmond, formerly Matt's British Pub and nicknamed "Matty's Pub", "Maddy's Pub" and "Manny's Pub" by Chris. Bob worked there as a sing-along leader in the late 1970s,[19] and it is here where he would later meet and fall in love with Barbara.[20] The pub closed in 2004.[21]

Mission BBQ
An American barbeque restaurant chain based outside of Baltimore, with stores in 16 states, including the Richmond area.
Chris mentioned in a February 2024 stream that he went to Mission BBQ a few times during his stay at Gateway Homes in Chesterfield County.
O'Toole's
An Irish pub Chris briefly referenced in his KCWC broadcast from 1998, with two locations in Midlothian and Richmond.

TGI Fridays
TGI Fridays is a casual dining chain where Chris used to hang out with his friend Joshua Martinez. Chris usually paid for both of their meals. On one occasion Chris had no money for food, so Josh paid for his own meal and ate while Chris looked on in envy.[36]
As of 2013 Chris would still go to TGI Fridays on occasion, even though he and Josh had long ceased to be friends. He claimed in an email to Kim that it was the only bar-like establishment which he still patronized at the time, although he did not make it clear whether he came there to drink or merely to eat.[37]
The chain's Charlottesville location, probably the one which Chris frequented, has since closed.
Tropical Smoothie Cafe
Tropical Smoothie Cafe is a restaurant franchise in the entire United States. Chris has mentioned it on at least two occasions, talking about its wraps, smoothies, and flatbread sandwiches with chips, etc. Chris said his friend Joshua Martinez introduced him to Tropical Cafe.[38][39]
The franchise has at least two locations in Charlottesville.
Twisted Branch Tea Bazaar
A restaurant specializing in tea and vegetarian meals, located in the Charlottesville, VA downtown mall.
LoveYouLongTime, whose friend wanted to meet with Chris, set up a group meeting here circa 2018. She told the story of the meeting on Kiwi Farms two years later, mentioning Chris's bizarre fashion, him introducing Magi-Chan and Mewtwo as his spouses while indicating at empty chairs, attempting to show off his electric powers, and dumping his bag of magic stones on the table to ramble about each of them for over half an hour.[40]
Wood Grill Buffet
A buffet chain with a location in Charlottesville. Despite being a buffet restaurant, Chris seems to think of the place as a high-quality eatery.
In an email to one of LoveYouLongTime's personas from November 2009, Chris mentions that his parents stopped at Wood Grill Buffet to buy dressing.[41]
In a Facebook post from January 2014, Chris asks for gift cards for restaurants such as Wood Grill Buffet, iHop, McDonald's and Burger King. He then disparages the latter two restaurants, claiming that they are "last choices" and that he prefers "good food".[42]
In an October 2019 chat in The Place Discord server, Chris mentions he and Barb went to Wood Grill Buffet for lunch on her birthday.[43]
