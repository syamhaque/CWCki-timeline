Delusion disclosure: This article or section takes place during Chris's phase of role-playing as Sonichu. He may refer to his Christine Chandler personality and physical body in the third person by using terms such as "mama".
Chris in September 2020
September was the ninth month of 2020. Chris spent most of the month answering questions through Discord. Chris also uploaded a short video with Barb to show that she is fine, and a video of Barb addressing Cole Smithey. At this point in time, it had been half a year since the COVID-19 pandemic had started spreading through the United States.

Highlights
2 September
The two debt cases from January wrap up; Chris owes $2,773.56 to one of his creditors and $905.36 to the other.
In a message to a Watchman, Chris discusses his debt hearing, Transformers toys, and the Dimensional Merge.[1]
3 September - The first of the Anaxis DMs.
4 September
Chris has Barbara record a video saying she's fine.[2]
Chris records a reaction video to the fourth part of TF2 Analysis's Red Vs Blue brony cartoon.[3]
Chris tells Anaxis about his wish for a nearby house for Naught and MKRNightVee to move into together so that the three can hang out, and complains about Barbara's raising of dogs Clover and Snoopy, saying that the dogs being kept inside makes it difficult to have visitors over.[4]
6 September
The nine-year anniversary of Bob's passing.[5]
Chris compares several characters from anime series The Melancholy of Haruhi Suzumiya to himself, Barbara, and some Watchmen members (comparing himself and Barbara to characters Haruhi and Kyon - who share a kissing scene in the series).[6]
7 September - Chris orders Naught to fulfill his outstanding orders for him and sends Naught screenshots of his customers' information.[7]
8 September
Chris approves a suggestion by klop to sell Sonichu comics on Amazon.[8]
Chris expresses feelings of loneliness, using his ongoing role-play as Sonichu and lamenting that Rosechu is stuck on the other side of the Iron Curtain.[8]
Chris receives a Luan Loud plush in the mail.[9]
Chris answers Discord Q&A 5.
9 September - Chris continues to answer the questions.
11 September - Chris responds to Discord Q&A 6.
15 September - Chris answers Discord Q&A 7.
16 September
Chris complains of spikes in blood pressure.[10]
The first of the released The Journey to the Merge Discord caps takes place.
20 September
In spite of having been trespassed from the property years prior, Chris goes to the Charlottesville Fashion Square.[11]
Chris chats in The Place about his obsessive thoughts on Megan Schroeder.[12]
22 September - Chris informs members of The Place about Praetor. He has lunch with Praetor members to discuss business.[13]
24 September - Chris responds to Discord Q&A 8.
26 September - Chris angrily answers a text from a disgruntled customer, Justin, regarding Chris's failure to send comics which had been purchased by Justin.[14]
27 September - Chris posts a set of screenshots from Justin DMs in The Place Discord server, complaining of customer Justin's hostile remarks. Chris does not censor the screenshots, doxing Justin's personal information to server members, and encourages them to dig into Justin's background.[15]
29 September - Chris experiences a high heart rate and is advised to see a doctor, to which Chris agrees.[16]
30 September
Chris releases To Cole Smithey, from Barb, and Sonichu (Prime), a video in which his mother addresses his half-brother Cole.
Chris says he has seen his doctor and that his pulse, blood pressure and EKG returned normal results.[17]
