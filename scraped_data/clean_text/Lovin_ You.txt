"Lovin' You" is the sixth track from the second album of Chris's cruddy musical project, Christian and the Hedgehog Boys COMEBACK!. This song was also played on the video Chris Sings Lovin' You.
In this song, Chris attempts to do an a cappella cover of "Lovin' You" by Minnie Riperton. However, due to a lack of practice, sheer laziness... or both, Chris gets the tune completely wrong. Some have even stated his vocals on here are "like a dying animal", which is far from off the mark.
