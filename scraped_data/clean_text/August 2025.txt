Notable events
1 August
Chris uploads Light Powered Cwclight Reviews - Gwenmi Mikoto.
Chris and Flutter are allegedly spotted back in the United States by Twitter user shizuearts,[1][2] at a Walmart in Lynchburg.[3]
5 August or before - Chris updates Transformers Legacy United Merge Bios on Cwclight, adding C-C 82 (Chris-Chan Origin Form), Gewnmi [sic] Mikoto, Decepticlone S.A.V. (Scoundrel As Vessel), Crackder (The First), Decepticlone Punislav, (Combiner Mode) Excelina, (Combiner Mode) Chris-Chan Prime, Focus, Tork, Stallion and Sparky to the list.
7 August - Chris finishes watching the 14th season of King of the Hill.[4]
8 August - Chris posts Light Powered Cwclight Reviews - Decepticlone S.A.V. (Scoundrel As Vessel).
9 August - Chris makes a short post to solemnly swear that—from this point forward—he will never be a jerk again.[5]
13 August - Chris and Flutter are spotted at a Walmart in Lynchburg.[6]
15 August - Chris uploads Light Powered Cwclight Reviews - Crackder the First.
22 August - Chris uploads Light Powered Cwclight Reviews - Decepticlone Punislav... yay.
29 August
SEGA opens a free four-day-long network test demo for their upcoming video game Sonic Racing: CrossWorlds.[7]
Chris uploads Light Powered Cwclight Reviews - Combiner Mode Excelina.
30 August
Chris alerts SEGA on Twitter about experiencing bugs while playing the network test demo for Sonic Racing: CrossWorlds.[8]
Chris teases on Twitter that "something really awesome" will happen in two weeks, it ended up being a stupid music video about his Transformers toys.[9]
31 August - Chris and presumably Flutter are spotted eating at the Red Lobster in Lynchburg.[10]
