The My Little Pony Protests are a nonviolent series of tweets, videos and other media with the intention of persuading Hasbro‚Äîthe owners of the My Little Pony franchise‚Äîto continue producing five more seasons of their animated series, Friendship is Magic in lieu of rebooting the franchise into its fifth generation, and from 2019 to 2020, to cancel the spin-off series, My Little Pony: Pony Life.
Chris, of course, remains the only active participant since August 2018. Being quite the big shot in C-197, he has conveyed that the aims of the protests are a means of preserving his imaginary friends in the Pony realm of Equestria; or simply put to everyone else, he refuses to accept that his favorite cartoon has concluded without his approval.
Having become seasoned in campaigns, such as the "Sonic Boom Protests" and #HarassCWCdirectly, the Hasbro Protests are one of Chris's most out of touch ventures to date, with him repeatedly demonstrating a very limited understanding of how animated shows like My Little Pony are actually produced. Fortunately, in his own best interests, he is proceeding with these protests in a far more restrained manner than previous endeavors.

Background
Main articles: My Little Pony and Chris and reality
An example of Chris's chronicles in Equestria, which he perceives as reality.
My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic was the fourth generation (or G4) of the Hasbro property, which aired from 10 October 2010 to 12 October 2019;[2] by definition, it was Chris's favorite show of the 2010s. His history with the franchise
is a particularly rich one: he initially got invested with the franchise to get closer with Megan, but he was still a fan even long after she stopped speaking with him. Years later, Chris attended three BronyCon conventions, his Mary Sue OC, Night Star, would partly take on the mantle of his adventures in Equestria, and he was publicly ostracized by several members of the brony analyst community.
Made especially clear after the Idea Guys' manipulations in early 2018, when faced with injustices in the real world, one of Chris's main coping mechanisms is escaping to his fantasy world of C-197, where he is an omnipotent, revered deity, and his denizens are various OCs and other fictional characters. According to Chris, in Equestria, he is an Alicorn‚Äîa Pegasus/Unicorn hybrid usually attributed to almighty characters in the series‚Äîand
has a strong rapport with the main characters, in addition to Night Star.[3][4] Elements of his delusions frequently seep back into reality, which is how articles such as these come to exist in the first place.
Rumors of a fifth generation were appearing as early as January 2018;[5] in due course, they caught the attention of Chris. To him, the end of Friendship is Magic is not just the conclusion of his favorite contemporary cartoon, but the end of his sporadic social life in safe spaces such as BronyCon, among other gatherings stemming from the series. Inwardly, he interprets the end of G4 as a threat to his airtight coping mechanism, as he claims the shift to a new canon in the form of G5 would see the perpetual stagnation or demise of C-197.[6][7]
Weeks prior to his opening protest, Chris attended BronyCon 2018, where the staff announced that 2019 would see their final convention.[8] This, compounded with the looming dread of G4 ending, may have been the catalyst for him to take arms and get Hasbro's attention. For Chris, Hasbro not succumbing to his demands would be to defy his self-proclaimed divinity. Though his decision to campaign was purely of his own accord, he asserts that the continuation of G4 was demanded by the highest order in Equestria ‚Äì the Princesses of Equestria.[9]
Protest beginnings
On 13 August 2018, Chris commenced his campaign with a statement posted via Twitter.[10]
In his opening statement, Chris declared that he had foreseen a possible future timeline in C-197, in which a spell intended to ascend Applejack (who was in G4 an "Earth Pony", ie. a normal horse that's neither a Pegasus nor a Unicorn) to become an Alicorn backfires, causing the incumbent Alicorn, Twilight Sparkle, to transfer her wings and horn to Pinkie Pie (who was in G4 an Earth Pony) and Fluttershy (who was in G4 a Pegasus), respectively; this was apparently where G4 would transition to G5.
On a more tangible note, Chris seems to have based his concerns on a single concept image for G5, which may or may not be official anyway.[5] Chris's delusional theory falls flat, considering that the alleged G5 art of Fluttershy has removed her wings.

Throughout the following few months leading up to G5's premiere in September, Chris would continue to enact various forms of protest. The most notable of which being him once again claiming that the world would end when G5 was released,[30] and that he would supposedly be willing to pay Hasbro one million dollars to convince them to cancel it.[31] During sometime in the middle of July 2021, leading up to Chris's planned trip to the Everfree Northwest My Little Pony convention, Chris recorded a statement for Fiona, promising her that he would not get angry in front of her when they would inevitably come into contact with pieces of merchandise or toys from Generation 5 at the convention, claiming to have "a good sense of self and self-control."[32]
Later on July 27th, Chris recorded and uploaded a video to YouTube entitled Direct Message to Hasbro: MLPG5 Is Cancelled. In it, he angrily shouts into the camera demanding that Hasbro cancel the production of MLP G5 and continue Friendship is Magic for five more seasons. In the background of the video, multiple punched-in holes in a wall can be seen. Although Chris stated in a Discord message that he had initially been punching the wall for other reasons,[33] it's still entirely possible that at least some of the damage dealt to the wall was simply done out of his anger for Generation 5.

After getting the soda and water, I took a lap around the store as I like to do. While onlookers during this time were initially patiently anticipating a possible public meltdown from Chris due to G5's inevitable presence at Everfree Northwest, those prospects were all immediately thrown out the window when Chris was arrested in the beginning of August 2021 on charges of engaging in incestuous behavior with his mother, Barbara. As a result of Chris being preoccupied with numerous unrelated exploits during his stay in jail, his previously active protests would enter a state of silence for over the next two years as the fifth generation of My Little Pony was being released.
Post-Jail Protests
After Chris was released in March 2023 and fully returned to the internet in October 2023, he would resume his protest where he left off in 2021. Only this time, in renewing his outcry, he has taken his acts of protest and intertwined them with his Neo Spiritual Christianity and Messiah complex. Since February 2024 he has engaged in a bizarre campaign of ongoing escalation on social media, first by blocking the majority of G5's voice cast on Twitter, then by directly suggesting that MLPG5 - and Pony Life before it - were Russian creations designed to destabilise and destroy G4, preceding this with the utterly bewildering claim that G5 was responsible for 'instigating' the ongoing Russo-Ukrainian War. As seen in the past, Chris can't handle changes to his beloved fictional characters. However, if this behavior goes unchecked, it could be only a matter of time before history repeats itself if he chooses to take matters into his own hands.
