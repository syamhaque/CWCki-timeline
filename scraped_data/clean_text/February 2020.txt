Chris in Feb 2020
Chris profile
February was the second month of 2020. Chris read more fanfiction about himself and ProjectSNT this month. He also watched the Sonic the Hedgehog movie, stating that he enjoyed it.

Highlights
1 February - Chris begs on Twitter for a Nintendo Switch copy of Pokémon Shield for his birthday.[1]
7 February - James Marsden, the actor playing Tom Wachowski in the Sonic the Hedgehog movie, is quizzed on IGN on his Sonic trivia knowledge, one question being whether Sonichu is a unlockable character in Sonic Forces.[2]
11 February
Chris, continuing to attempt to get his foot in the door regarding ProjectSNT, livestreams himself reading SNT Vs Sonichu, an edgy fanfic by Opuscon789 involving him and SNT,[3]
Chris acknowledges the stuff Multidimensional White Knights did as true in his mind. This was done in the Arbitarch CWC Alliance Discord server.[4]
14 February - Sonic the Hedgehog premieres in U.S. theaters, and Chris goes to watch it. Despite its titular character's blue arms, he enjoys it and recommends it on Twitter.[citation needed]
22 February - In response to a Twitter user posting about his sibling being horrified at hearing parental sex, Chris relates by bringing up a traumatic memory from his past: seeing his father's penis.[5]
23 February - After weens replied to Chris's tweets from the 22nd, Chris wrote a screed saying the trolls made him hate sex and gave him PTSD, and that they'll be "going down in all of the coming and pending events taking place".[5]
24 February - Christian Love Day: Chris turns 38.
29 February - Xleepy releases a video of Chris demonstrating his psychic powers.[6]
