The list
The 69 Destined Sonichus and Rosechus is a list dictated to Chris by the Idea Guys. As befitting their style, it contains a number of edgy references to infamous people, as well as a reference to the number 69 - a number associated with mutual oral sex. Originally, the lore was that any further Sonichus and Rosechus made after would not affect the list number, as "those children did not originate directly from the Chaotic Rainbow of February 1, 2003."[1] However, months later during the Guard Dogs saga, Chris added J-chu to the list, counting it as #70.[2]
Chris maintained belief in the legitimacy of this list after the Idea Guys had been removed from power, posting it to Facebook three months later.[3] He also mentioned the list on video in Nightstar is a very important Special Sonichu.
