Chris's emotional state this month.
November was the eleventh month of 2013. The month was marked by Chris going on several rants followed by half-assed apologies throughout the month, mostly involving Megan.
Events of November 2013
1 November - A male high school classmate emails Chris to tell him to get over his his heartbreak regarding his former gal pals. Chris will not be consoled.
4 November
Chris posts to Facebook asking the world to ignore his crudely drawn pornography. He offers rationalizations and recriminations concerning ShecameforCWC.jpg.
Chris publicly apologizes to Megan for the drawing.
5 November - Chris posts CWC Lego Manchester High School Oct 18 2013 on YouTube, his first video since 2011. He posts photos of the building on Facebook.
6 November - Chris posts on Facebook that he wants Lego's attention. It is also revealed that his video is part of another contest.
7 November - Chris comments that a drawing of Anna by William is cute.
8 November - Chris apologizes to his Facebook audience for accidentally sending a mass message about his Lego project.
12 November
A high school classmate emails Chris to say he was shocked to discover one of his old videos. Chris responds that he is a complete wreck.
Chris posts another apology on Facebook.
15 November - Release of the life upgrade, upgraded: the PS4.
19 November - Chris uploads pictures of his heart, crushed by Sarah Bevel and his other false and dishonest gal-pals, along with a sort of poem, on Facebook.
22 November - Release of the HEXbox One.
23 November - Chris uses some of his monthly tugboat to get "So Need a Cute Girl" up on Amazon's MP3 store.
25 November - Chris posts 3DS friend codes for himself and, apparently, his mother, seeking connections with other Pokémon players.
26 November - Chris apologizes to Megan. Again. He also mopes some more about his online reputation.
29 November
Chris emails his friend Rebeckah to ask for Sarah Bevel's email so that his mother can talk to her.
Chris posts a Thanksgiving message that he is extremely depressed.
30 November - Chris angrily denies that he and his mother had Thanksgiving dinner at a soup hotel.
