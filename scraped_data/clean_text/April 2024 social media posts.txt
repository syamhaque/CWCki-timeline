On Leaked Chats
14 April
2:59 PM
I am reminded of when Susan started out on her "Comedy Gigs" on Seinfeld, and she just relayed everything that Jerry had said to her on the voicemail as part of her gig.
That was very wrong, because things like Voicemails, or E-Mails, about something personal between the two individuals are not to be shared publicly, especially in mockery or jest.
The one who does this does the worst damage to themselves.
