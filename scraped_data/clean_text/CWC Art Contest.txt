Round 1
A censored example of an email message Chris revealed.
CWC ART CONEST Round 1 is a video uploaded to Chris's YouTube channel on 13 February 2024 which involved Chris screen-recording his gmail screen while opening fanart pictures. Chris carelessly revealed email messages - which includes information such as names and email addresses - while looking through the pictures. One of the artworks has Caden's dox superimposed on it.
A censored screenshot of Caden's info superimposed on fanart.
The video was taken down a few days after uploading "due to a privacy claim by a third party". The original video is mirrored via GhostArchive[2] and catbox.moe.[3]

Doxxing
A redditor warns contestants via email that they have been doxxed.
During the video, while looking through the submissions, Chris revealed private information about some contestants, such as their names and email addresses.
Users of the subreddit r/ChrisChanSonichu commented[4] upon this, saying:
smoodoos1
Chris said he wouldn't dox anyone; however, in his video, he proceeded to not censor several email addresses.
Tigs1112
I bet the weens are going to spam those uncensored emails.
TsukasaElkKite
I politely emailed them letting them know Chris doxxed them and that they should report the video.
Warning Emails
The Reddit user TsukasaElkKite emailed at least two of the doxxed individuals, warning them and encouraging them to report the video for privacy violation. It is unclear if these warnings are what led to the taking down of the video. However, the video was unavailable until the 20th of March 2024 and listed the reason as "This video is no longer available due to a privacy claim by a third party"[5].
