Notable events
1 September - Chris spots an imposter while playing the network test demo for Sonic Racing: CrossWorlds.[1]
5 September - Chris uploads Light Powered Cwclight Reviews - Combiner Mode Chris-Chan Prime.
7 September - Chris is spotted at Valley View Mall in Roanoke, VA.[2]
8 September - Chris is allegedly spotted at the DMV in Lynchburg, VA.[3]
12 September
Chris uploads Light Powered Cwclight Reviews - Punicrackder V plus Music Video.
Chris uploads Go Transformers - Theme of Transformers of Cwcville - Music Video.
13 September - Chris replied to a commenter on his previous video, marking the first time Chris made a comment in the comment section on his own video.[4]
14 September - Chris addresses the Sonichu Medallion Curse.[5]
18 September
Chris replies to more commenters on Go Transformers - Theme of Transformers of Cwcville - Music Video.
Chris creates a new eBay account[6] to sell his Xbox One X,[7] and he also puts up a listing for it on Cwclight.[8][9] It was sold eight hours later.[10]
Chris uploads Retro Review: Titans Return Son-Chu Review, a revised version of Son-Chu Review.
Chris uploads OG Sonichu Medallion Upgraded, Rebirthed and Redeemed.
19 September
Chris replies to a commenter on OG Sonichu Medallion Upgraded, Rebirthed and Redeemed.
Chris uploads Light Powered Cwclight Reviews - (Renegade) Focus.
20 September
Chris uploads two clips from OG Sonichu Medallion Upgraded, Rebirthed and Redeemed.
Chris uploads Punicrackder-V Review Original Ending and Punicrackde- V Supplement/Outtake Plus Unedited Ending.
24 September - Chris uploads Speaking Akashic.
25 September - Chris is spotted at a Sheetz in Lynchburg, VA.[11]
26 September - Chris uploads Light Powered Cwclight Reviews - Tork.
29 September - Chris uploads PSA: Ignorance Is Truly Bliss Against The Haters And Fakers.
