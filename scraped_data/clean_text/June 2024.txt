Chris in June.
Chris and Flutter in June.
June was the sixth month of 2024. Chris promoted his Minecraft server, on Twitter and in a livestream, and spent time with Flutter.

Notable events
1 June - Chris was seen online on Discord through 8:16 PM - 9:00 PM EST.
2 June - Chris was seen online on Discord through 2:09 PM - 2:14 PM and 9:07 PM - 10:36 PM EST.
4 June - Chris was seen online on Discord through 3:36 PM - 4:32 PM and 8:43 PM - 11:40 PM EST.
5 June - Chris was seen online on Discord through 3:47 PM - 4:43 PM EST.
15 June - Chris makes a rambling post apologizing for his lack of social media activity.[1]
16 June - Chris teases an upcoming livestream on Minecraft.[2]
16 June - Chris changes his Minecraft skin on the ChristineWeston account.[3]
27 June - The three-year anniversary of the beginning of Chris's affair with his own mother.
29 June - Chris and Flutter are spotted at River Ridge Mall.[4]
30 June - Chris livestreams Christ Chan Live Stream: OG Cwcville (Cwcville)-60 Minecraft.
