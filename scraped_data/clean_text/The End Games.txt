The store has been around since at least 1999,[2] and Chris made occasional references and visits to it over the years, but he didn't truly begin going to it until around 2015, where he'd frequent the store until his ban in early 2018. Chris enjoyed playing PokÃ©mon here, and made a few adult friends from the PokÃ©mon League. It is located about 10 miles away from 14 Branchland Court.
