Title: November 2011
URL: https://sonichu.com/cwcki/November_2011
Categories: Category, Chronology
================================================================================

Robee Sonee and his bandmates release their new album.
November was the penultimate month of 2011. It saw more fallout from the 28 October 2011 incident, with Chris and Barbara both being brought into court for the fiasco.
Events
November - Thorg holds a Q&A.
1 November - Michael Snyder files a civil suit against both Chris and Barb, four days after the GAMe PLACe incident.
4 November - In a series of edits, Chris releases photographs of Michael Snyder from his latest visit to the GAMe PLACe to the CWCki.
7 November - Chris goes to court to answer charges of trespass, misdemeanor assault and failure to stop at the scene of an accident. None of the charges are dropped.
12 November - The debut album of Robee Sonee and the Tomgirls, Chris's latest fictional band, is leaked.
19 November - A voice recording of a
personal dating ad that Chris made three years ago is finally discovered.
22 November - Two prank calls are released by TheCWCvilleLibrary, who received them from an anonymous source. Ironically, one of the calls (made with a Chris soundboard) was made to the GAMe PLACe on 27 October 2011.
25 November - Chris posts in the ModNation Racers forum in the hope of getting custom car of the week.
