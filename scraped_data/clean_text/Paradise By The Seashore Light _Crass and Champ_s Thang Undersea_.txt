Title: Paradise By The Seashore Light (Crass and Champ's Thang Undersea)

Categories: Christian and the Hedgehog Boys COMEBACK!, Ivy Saga, Music
================================================================================

"Paradise By The Seashore Light (Crass and Champ's Thang Undersea)" is Christian and the Hedgehog Boys's version of Meat Loaf's "Paradise by the Dashboard Light", dedicated to Ivy's pet hermit crabs, Crass and Champ. It is the seventh track from Christian and the Hedgehog Boys COMEBACK!.
Unlike Chris's previous work, where he wrote new lyrics, this time he just sings the original song word-for-word, adding the names of the crabs intermittently to identify them as the male and female characters in the song's story, in addition to a few minor references to the sea. Ivy said that this laziness is because "Paradise..." was one of the last songs Chris wrote and he was getting bored. His inability to keep both in rhythm and in tune with the song is outstanding.
Notable variations from the Meat Loaf version are that Chris omits the entire Phil Rizzuto baseball section of the song, is incapable of singing the faster parts and frequently cannot distinguish between the male and female voices. (Sound familiar?) He sings in a boring monotone when he realizes it's the boy's part, and a screeching falsetto when he realizes it's the girl's part. Even funnier, Chris believes that Crass and Champ were male and female, respectively, even though Ivy told him that the two were male.
