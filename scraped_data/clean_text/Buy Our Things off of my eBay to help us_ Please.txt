Buy Our Things off of my eBay to help us, Please is a video made on 9 April 2018. In it, Chris aims his camera phone to Barb and gets her to announce more of Cole's comics for sale on eBay. This is fifth time where Barb starred in a begging video.
The video was set to Private at some point around 21 November 2023,[1] however a copy was archived.
