Meghan Kathleen
Ringo,[14][15][8] more commonly known as MKRNightVee (MKR for short), CPUMagentaHeart, and sometimes referred to as Maker by Chris, appeared in December 2018 and was active as a white knight and fantasy enabler on Twitter and Discord, until Chris cut ties with her in July 2021 citing her toxic behavior.[8] She got into contact with Chris through Twitter,[16] then a friend looking up Chris's phone number for her.[17] She presented herself to Chris as being the CPU goddess of the Nintendo Switch, offering him validation on his fantasies of being a CPU and having magic powers. She has claimed that "My goal isnt to feed into his delusions so much as it is to gently offer kind advice and be there as a shoulder to lean on for moral support [...] I'm trying to help him better himself".[17] It is unclear how MKR thought encouraging Chris's delusions with Hyperdimension Neptunia CPU lore would lead to him becoming better.
MKR acted notably possessive towards Chris. She created or spread lies about several other people interacting with Chris, even to the extent of falsely accusing them of criminal activity. She also became irritated with Chris when she felt that he was ignoring her,[18] and reacted with anger when Chris decided to leave her group's Discord server The Place due to toxicity from her and others in the group. MKR attempted to emotionally manipulate Chris into returning, but he stood his ground and insisted she work on improving herself first.[8]

Interactions with Chris
MKR set up a Discord group for Chris, telling her friends from The Sons of the Miscreants Discord server to role-play as CPU goddesses to gain Chris's trust, while also instituting a no-trolling rule.[9]
Under her assistance, Owls and would-be Watchmen members would roleplay as goddesses while in contact with Chris. MKR has claimed to be doing it out of the belief that doing so will somehow "better" him.
Tings Tingz comments on her concern after seeing one of MKR's tweets.
When MKR first appeared on the scene, Tings Tingz was suspicious of her due to MKR being an enabler to Chris's delusions. Tings was particularly suspicious of MKR's usage of the Hyperdimension Neptunia CPU lore to gain Chris's attention - as the Idea Guys had pioneered the use of the lore to manipulate Chris. Tings reached out to Chris with her suspicions, to which Chris defended MKR.[19]
On 21 January 2019, a Kiwi Farms thread was posted about MKR. The thread included several pages of chatlogs from The Sons of the Miscreants Discord server, in which MKR had discussed her interactions with Chris and recruited Discord users to chat with him.[14] Tings Tingz pointed out the thread to Chris and warned him of MKR's "belittling" and "mocking" comments about him in the chats, her usage of the CPU lore to trick him, and also alleging MKR and her friends to be trolls and advising Chris to cut contact. Chris responded, "My head hurts." Tings pressed further, asking him, "Your still following MKR. Are you choosing the reassuring lie?" Chris answered, "I'm talking to her about the issue; I have not completely processed the details yet!"[20] Tings also confronted MKR.[21]

In August 2020, Chris offered MKR several of his possessions in his Last Will and Testament.
On 4 January 2021, Chris bought a Funko Pop figure of Transformers character Starscream, then mentioned to MKR that he thought it was something she would like and asked for confirmation of her address. MKR replied that the figure was cute and sent her address details.[22]

On 5 March 2021, MKR lashed out at Chris over Twitter in a fit of jealousy.
A month prior, MKR had learned that Chris was planning to use a taser on his quartz crystals in an attempt to activate the Dimensional Merge, which his new friend group Praetor was to let him borrow. MKR, among other Watchmen, took to spreading a vicious rumor on the planned taser usage.[4]
Though nearly a month had passed, on 5 March, MKR sent an angry Tweet directed towards Chris which expressed her disdain.[32]
She then temporarily deleted her Twitter account a few days later. However, this was a ruse by MKR designed to manipulate the public and Praetor - MKR believed that by dramatically acting as if she were quitting on Chris, it would lull Praetor into a false sense of security,[33] although Praetor members have not been known to care.
She also claimed to the other Watchmen that her Twitter account's true intent was to draw the attention of weens in order to put them on full blast.[18]
She reactivated her account, and tweeted again to Chris in April, replying to Chris tagging her to show her a gif file,[34] and later to reply to another of Chris's tweets in order to call one of his enablers a "faggot".[35]
MKR attempted to manipulate Chris into interacting with Alexander Jahans, another delusional lolcow which she and Naught had taken interest in.[26] In March 2021, when Chris was chatting with The Place about his psychic detective roleplay, MKR claimed that Jahans's abilities could help.[3] Behind Chris's back, MKR rationalized the setup as "two tards playing pretend in a controlled environment."[26] However, Jahans did not appear.
Chris cuts ties
Main article: Watchmen infighting drama
In June 2021, internal conflict arose among Watchmen members over Naught doxing The WCT which resulted in the group separating. MKR had sided with Naught and Chris against, leading to Chris distancing himself from MKR in the fallout. MKR attempted to emotionally manipulate Chris into returning to her side. On 29 June, MKR tagged Chris‚Äôs Twitter and posted, ‚ÄúYour obviously good at using people and then moving on to the next. Have fun getting fucked over by WCT and Praetor.‚Äù[36] She followed up by tweeting that she regretted having spent her time drawing art for Chris and accused him of being unappreciative of it.[37]
On 26 June 2021, Chris left the Watchmen Discord server The Place. He and MKR discussed the matter over texts from 1-3 July. Chris mentioned that he feels her, Naught, and Kyle were toxic, after hearing edgy comments regarding racism and Nazis in voice chats and movie nights and therefore dubbed all three of them to be part of "the toxic trio". Chris also complained about Naught's attempts at doxing both Spunky and The WCT for not allowing him to use their Kiwi Farms accounts.
Chris ended with a request for the "toxic trio" to
"shape up, clear themselves up spiritually, let go of their respective egos, and become most genuinely enlightened to fully comprehend", otherwise Chris would not return to The Place. Meghan tried to guilt-trip Chris into returning to her by telling him that she felt that her image has been ruined thanks to Chris, and how she feels "betrayed and hurt". MKR then said she "can only become better if you help me, I need you in my life," to which Chris stood his ground and responded that only she can help herself.[38]
