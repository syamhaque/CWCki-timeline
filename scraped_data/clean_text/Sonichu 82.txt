Sonichu #82 is an unreleased issue of the Sonichu comic series. To date, only the cover has been released. The cover was released within a batch of artwork Chris posted to his NightStarRosechu2891 DeviantART account. Notably, the cover date of April 2023 takes place after Chris's release from Central Virginia Regional Jail on his incest charge. The cover's signature reads "Jesus Christ Chan Sonichu Prime", and the corner box contains the initials JCWCSP - short for Jesus Christine Weston Chandler Sonichu Prime - proving that Chris's messiah complex which ramped up during his stay in jail has continued unabated.
Chris has decided to number the issue "82", most likely as a reference to his birth year of 1982.
