Chris Chan creepy smile or DSCF0426.AVI was a short 2 second video recorded on 22 August 2009, likely by Kim Wilson. CWCki editor Riley Martin uploaded a screencap of the video on 30 March 2011 to the site called Chris in his Escort creepy smile.jpg. However, the video would not surface until over a decade later on 10 October 2025, when YouTube user Statustjej finally uploaded it. Statustjej also later shared a photo from the same day, which wasn't previously known to the wider public.
