Chris in April 2020
April was the fourth month of 2020. This month began in the midst of COVID-19 being declared a national emergency. This caused Chris to claim that he had swapped bodies with Sonichu, leading Chris to a new form of escapism that lasted until February 2021.

Highlights
6 April - Chris shares a video from ILoveKimPossibleaLot.[1] The rest of his Twitter posts during the week are generated tweets showing off his "biggest fans".
10 April - Chris posts an update after 4 days of inactivity,[2] and states he will stream live reactions for every episode of TF2 Analysis Anarchy;[3] the first of Red vs Blue Reaction Livestreams was streamed later that day.
Image from Chris's pony calendar, showing a homage to Revolutionary Girl Utena.
15 April - Chris announces that he had body-swapped with Sonichu – an elaborate maladaptive coping method for missing out on the BABScon convention which had been cancelled due to COVID-19.[4]
19 April - Chris is interviewed live by Leftovermedia.
20 April - Chris posts about his birthday, confirming the Idea Guy retcons.[citation needed]
25 April - Chris makes a lengthy Twitter thread about Nowacking.[citation needed]
29 April
The Creamarina Twitter account is discussed in a conversation between Chris roleplaying as Sonichu and Midnight Moonflower.[5]
Chris raises the prices of the magazines, due to a rise in Lulu fees.[citation needed]
30 April - Chris comments on a video of lightning and a rainbow, apparently viewing it as a Dimensional Portal of some kind.[citation needed]
