Notable events
2 November - Chris responds to Smiling Friends referencing him.[1]
3 November - Chris creates a Twitter poll to see how many people like the Light Powered Cwclight Reviews series.[2]
4 November
Gubernatorial election in Virginia.
Chris reveals Bob's last words to him.[3]
6 November - Chris updates Transformers Legacy United Merge Bios on Cwclight again, adding Autobot Soldier Clyde to the list.
7 November - Chris publishes Light Powered Cwclight Reviews - Sparky.
8 November
Chris publishes JCWCSP's OLDER Transformers and Gobots Figures For Sale on Cwclight.
Chris publishes JCWCSP's older Transformers for sale‎‎.
14 November - Chris publishes Light Powered Cwclight Reviews - Autobot Soldier Clyde.
15 November
Chris publishes Go Transformers - Theme of Transformers of Cwcville - Updated Music Video. Chris then set the video to unlisted and then to private later in the day.
Chris publishes Go Transformers Music Video - Updated Plus Subtitles, but removed it shortly after.
18 November
Chris publishes Go Transformers - Theme of Transformers of Cwcville - Music Video Fully Updated and Go Transformers - Theme of Transformers of Cwcville - Music Video Fully Updated Plus Subtitles.
21 November
Chris publishes Cwclight Reviews Opening Intros with Subtitles and Cwclight Reviews Opening Intros.
Chris publishes JCWCSP Go Transformers - Theme of Transformers of Cwcville - Music Video Plus Subtitles. Chris then unlisted the video due to a mistake in it and reuploaded a new version.
Chris publishes JCWCSP Go Transformers - Theme of Transformers of Cwcville - Music Video. Originally titling it JCWCSP Go Transformers - Theme of Transformers of Cwcville - Music Video Plus Subtitles before correcting it. Chris then unlisted the video due to a mistake in it and reuploaded a new version.
22 November - Chris is spotted at a Target in Lynchburg, VA.[4]
25 November - Chris records a behind the scenes video of him editing a future video.[5]
27 November - Thanksgiving.
