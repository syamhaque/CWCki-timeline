Further Requested, Please is a video uploaded by Chris on 13 July 2016. It is, like the title suggests, another request for money. This time, however, Barb appears instead of Chris to do the begging, saying that the money is for her dental work.
This is notable for being the first video in which Barb plays a major role, however she has previously appeared in You're Always Pretty, and ChandlerFamilyChristmas2004.
